Ingredients of Pony's Winter Vegetable Hungarian Goulash

  1. Prepare 500 grams of Braising Beef Steak.
  2. Prepare 1 of Brown Onion.
  3. Prepare 1 cup of Diced Brown Potatoes.
  4. You need 1 cup of Chopped Cauliflower.
  5. Prepare 1/3 cup of Plain Flour.
  6. You need 1 tbsp of Paprika.
  7. You need 1 liter of Beef Stock.
  8. You need 1 clove of Garlic.
  9. Prepare 1 cup of Diced Pumkin or Sweet Potato.
  10. You need 1/2 cup of Tomato Puree.

Pony's Winter Vegetable Hungarian Goulash instructions

  1. Dice Onion & Garlic.
  2. Dice Braising Beef and coat in flour.
  3. In a large saucepan, heat oil..
  4. Add onion and garlic. Cook until translucent..
  5. Add flour coated diced beef. Cook to seal only..
  6. Add beef stock, paprika and tomato puree..
  7. Add diced winter vegetables. Mix until all covered..
  8. Simmer on low heat for 2.5 hours or until tenderly cooked stirring occassionally..
  9. Season with Salt & Pepper. Served best with Mashed Potatos!.
