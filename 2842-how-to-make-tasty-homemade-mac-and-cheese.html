You can cook Homemade mac and cheese using 9 ingredients and 5 steps. Here is how you cook it.

Ingredients of Homemade mac and cheese

  1. You need of Any kind of noodles.
  2. You need 1 of lot of garlic.
  3. It's 2 of teapoons of onion and parsley.
  4. You need of Salt and pepper.
  5. Prepare of For the cheese sauce.
  6. It's 1 (8 oz.) of cream cheese.
  7. You need 1 1/2 stick of butter.
  8. You need 2 cups of cheese.
  9. You need 1/2 cup of milk.

Homemade mac and cheese step by step

  1. Make the noodles. I like to season the noodles as well. So I put the salt and some garlic powder..
  2. Melt the butter and the cream cheese. Keep stirring you dont want it to stick or burn..
  3. Add the sauce to the noodles and add the garlic salt and pepper and stir the snot out of it... add more cheese to it and keep stirring it.... I like to keep the burner on low it helps the cheese to melt to it....
  4. Mix some of the cheese to it and stir it. And milk.
  5. Taste it to make sure it is the way you like it and boom done....
