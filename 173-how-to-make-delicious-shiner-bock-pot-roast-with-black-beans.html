Ingredients of shiner bock pot roast with black beans

  1. It's 2 1/2 lb of boneless beef chuck roast.
  2. It's 1 cup of dry black beans.
  3. You need 1 of onion, chopped.
  4. It's 1 medium of green bell pepper, chopped.
  5. Prepare 2 can of 10 oz diced tomatoes with green chilies.
  6. You need 1/4 cup of flour.
  7. You need 1/4 cup of bar-b-q seasoning.
  8. You need 1 tbsp of chopped garlic.
  9. It's 1 can of shiner bock beer.
  10. Prepare 2 tbsp of veg oil.

shiner bock pot roast with black beans step by step

  1. boil black beans with 2 cups water in a 1 qt sauce pan over med-high heat 2 minutes. remove from heat, cover and let stand 30 -60 minutes. meanwhile, chop onion, bell pepper and garlic and set aside..
  2. combine flour and bbq seasoning on a plate. cut roast into sections; trim away and discard fat. turn roast pieces in flour mix to coat..
  3. heat oil in a 5 qt pot or dutch oven over med heat 3 min. Sear beef 3 minutes per side to brown. Remove from pot and set aside..
  4. Add onion and garlic to pot; saute 3 minutes. Add tomatoes w/ grn chilies & beer, stirring to loosen any brown bits from bottom of pan. bring to a boil. drain beans and stir into bot. add beef and bell pepper..
  5. cover pot and reduce heat to med-low. cook 1 hour, then stir. cook 1 more hour, or until meat is fork-tender. stir occasionally, esp during last half hour of cooking. add 1/2 to 1 cup water if cooking liquid reduces too low (beans may stick and not cook properly.).
  6. break meat apart into chunks and shreds with fork or spoon. serve over rice or in flour tortillas..
  7. serve shiner bock pot roast with black beans over white rice or in. flour tortillas with jicama and orange salad..
