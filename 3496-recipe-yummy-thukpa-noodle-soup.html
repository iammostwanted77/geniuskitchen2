Ingredients of Thukpa (Noodle Soup)

  1. It's 1 cup of noodles / 1 small packet.
  2. It's 2 of chicken cubes, crumbled.
  3. It's 1 tbsp. of oil.
  4. It's 1 tsp. of garlic, chopped.
  5. Prepare 1 cup of assorted Bell Pepper, cubed.
  6. Prepare 1 of onion, cubed.
  7. Prepare 4-5 stalks of bok choy, roughly chopped.
  8. It's 1/2 tsp. of pepper powder.
  9. You need to taste of salt.
  10. Prepare 1/2 tsp. of red chilli powder.
  11. You need 1 tbsp. of red chilli sauce (opt).
  12. Prepare 1 of egg omelette, shredded.
  13. It's 1 tbsp. of spring onion, chopped.
  14. It's 1 tbsp. of lemon juice (opt).

Thukpa (Noodle Soup) instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saute the garlic till it changes colour. Add the chicken cubes, 3 1/2 cups water and bring it to a boil..
  2. Add the noodles and boil for a minute. Add the bell peppers, onion, salt, pepper, chilli powder and chilli sauce. Let it boil for a minute..
  3. Now add the bok choy and simmer further for a minute. Switch off the flame and add the lemon juice. Mix well..
  4. Serve in individual bowls. Garnish with the shredded omelette and spring onion..
