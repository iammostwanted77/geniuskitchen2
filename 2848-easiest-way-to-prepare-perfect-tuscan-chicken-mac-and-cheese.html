Ingredients of Tuscan Chicken Mac and Cheese

  1. Prepare 12 oz of box of elbow noodles.
  2. It's 3 of boneless skinless chicken breast.
  3. Prepare 2 cups of baby spinach.
  4. Prepare 2 cups of heavy cream.
  5. Prepare 1 cup of chicken broth.
  6. You need 1/2 cup of white wine.
  7. It's 6 oz of mushrooms chopped.
  8. You need 1 of onion diced.
  9. It's 2 cloves of garlic.
  10. You need 1 tsp of paprika.
  11. You need 1/2 tsp of Italian seasoning.
  12. You need 1/2 cup of grated parmesan.
  13. You need 1 cup of grated mozzarella.
  14. Prepare 1 cup of grated gruyere cheese.

Tuscan Chicken Mac and Cheese step by step

  1. Boil water for your noodles and cook according to box..
  2. Cover chicken in paprika and italian seasoning and cook through..
  3. Remove chicken and saute mushrioms, onions and garlic until soft..
  4. Add wine and simmer untill reduced by half. Add broth and cream and bring back to a simmer..
  5. Add spinach and cover to thicken a bit..
  6. Add cheeses, and stir. Add noodles and stir to coat the noodles..
  7. Slice chicken into bite size pieces and stir in. Serve hot..
