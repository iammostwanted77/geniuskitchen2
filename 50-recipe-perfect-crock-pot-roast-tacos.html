Crock pot roast tacos instructions

  1. Add roast to crock pot on medium or high heat.
  2. Add rotel to crock pot then fill can with water and add to crock pot..
  3. Add both packages of taco seasoning.
  4. Cover crock pot and cook for 8 hours on medium or 6 hours on high..
  5. 1 hour before finished take out roast and shred to pieces. Then return to crock pot for 1 more hour on low..
  6. Add to tacos, sit down and enjoy. Way better than regular ground beef tacos. :):).
