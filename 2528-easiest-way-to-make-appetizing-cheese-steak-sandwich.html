Cheese steak sandwich step by step

  1. Put the steak in a polythene bag, and hit it until it goes flat..
  2. Then add some salt and pepper on both sides..
  3. Slice the onion and peppers in to strips..
  4. Fry the onion for about 1 minute in a few teaspoons of oil..
  5. Then add the green pepper to the pan and add some salt and pepper..
  6. You will need to cook the onion/pepper mix until it's softened, this takes around 5-6 minutes..
  7. Push the onion/pepper mix to one side and add in the steak. Cook for about 1 minute each side..
  8. Turn the pan off, leave the steak to rest for a couple of minutes the slice in to strips..
  9. Start layering the panini breads with the mayonnaise, tomato and pepper mix..
  10. Add on the steak and cheese. Then put the sandwich under the grill for a couple of minutes, just so that the cheese melts..
  11. Eat it..
