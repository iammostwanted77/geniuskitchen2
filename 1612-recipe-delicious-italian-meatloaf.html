Italian Meatloaf instructions

  1. preheat oven 350*F coat 9x5 inch loaf pan with cooking spray.
  2. in large bowl,soak breadcrumbs in milk.
  3. in frying pan add oil cook onion about 7-10min. add celery cook 3-4more min.scrape mix into bowl of breadcrumbs..
  4. Add turkey beef,parmensan cheese,carrot puree,ketchup,salt and pepper and stir to combined.
  5. turn mix into loaf pan smooth top..
  6. spread timato sauce over the meatloaf and lay bacon on top..
  7. Bake 45-50 minutes intill center is not pink.
