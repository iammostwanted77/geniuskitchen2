Ingredients of Steamed ginger tilapia

  1. Prepare 1 tsp of coconut oil.
  2. You need 1 tbsp of leek, chopped.
  3. It's 5-6 of button mushrooms, sliced.
  4. It's 1 tsp of chopped fresh ginger.
  5. You need 1 tbsp of Bragg all purpose liquid seasoning.
  6. It's 1/4 cup of zucchini, sliced thinly.
  7. It's 5 of sugar peas, chopped.
  8. It's 1/2 cup of spinach.
  9. You need 1 of Tilapia filet.
  10. It's of Sesame seeds.
  11. It's of Sea salt.
  12. It's of Pepper.
  13. Prepare 1 tbsp of nutritional yeast.

Steamed ginger tilapia step by step

  1. In a pan melt 1 tsp coconut oil. Add leek, mushrooms and ginger cook 1 minute. Turn heat down to medium..
  2. Place fish over veggies. Sprinkle fish with salt, pepper, chili powder, and al purpose seasoning.
  3. Top fish with sugar peas, zucchini, cook 3 minutes. Top with spinach, cook 10 minutes or until fish is cooked through. This creates a top over your fish, covering it to steam. For quicker cooking add a top to pan..
