Ingredients of Manicotti With Eggplant Sauce

  1. You need 1 small of Eggplant, peeled and coarsely chopped.
  2. It's 1/2 cup of Chopped onion.
  3. It's 2 clove of Garlic, minced.
  4. You need 1/2 tsp of Dried tarragon.
  5. It's 1/4 tsp of Dried thyme.
  6. Prepare 1 can of No-salt-added diced tomatoes, undrained.
  7. It's 1 can of No-salt-added tomato sauce.
  8. You need 1 packages of Frozen chopped spinach, thawed and well drained.
  9. Prepare 1 cup of Reduced fat ricotta cheese.
  10. It's 1 cup of Shredded part-skim mozzarella cheese, divided.
  11. It's 1/2 cup of Egg substitute.
  12. Prepare 1/4 cup of Grated Parmesan cheese.
  13. It's 2 tbsp of Minced fresh parsley.
  14. Prepare 6 of Manicotti shells, cooked rinsed and drained..

Manicotti With Eggplant Sauce step by step

  1. In a large skillet coated with nonstick cooking sprays cook and stir the egg-plant, onion, garlic, tarragon and thyme until vegetables are tender. Add tomatoes and tomato sauce; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered for 3-4 minutes, set aside..
  2. In a large bowl, combine the spinach, ricotta, 1/2 cup mozzarella, egg substitute, Parmesan and parsley; mix well..
  3. Stuff into manicotti shells. Pale in an 11-in x 7-in x 2-in baking dish coated with nonstick cooking spray. Spoon eggplant sauce over manicotti; sprinkle with remaining mozzarella. Cover and bake at 350 for 25-30 minutes or. Until heated through..
