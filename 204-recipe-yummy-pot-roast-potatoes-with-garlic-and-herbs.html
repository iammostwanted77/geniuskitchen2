Pot roast potatoes with garlic and herbs step by step

  1. Rince the potatoes until they are clean. then boil them until they are soft but not over cooked..
  2. Drain water and let them cool down..
  3. Once cooled empty the pot the pour the margarine and put it back to the stove as the margarine is melting then pour all those herbs and garlic then add potatoes salt and pepper stir and toss.
  4. Then once your potatoes turn to be golden remove them from the stove your pot roast potatoes is done.
