Chicken chilli sauce step by step

  1. Place all the ingredients at kitchen counter..
  2. In chicken add chopped garlic, ginger & soya sauce..
  3. Also add soya sauce white & black pepper..
  4. Also add salt & marinate the chicken for 10 minutes..
  5. In a pan add oil chopped garlic fry little bit then add marinated chicken cook until it turned into white colour..
  6. Add chopped vegetables cook for some seconds then add white pepper..
  7. Also add black pepper salt & paprika..
  8. Also add oyster sauce, worcestershire sauce & ketchup..
  9. Mix well then add half cup water & spring onion..
  10. Dissolve cornflour with cold water and add to the chicken. It doesn't contain too kuch gravy..
  11. Chicken chilli sauce is ready to serve..
