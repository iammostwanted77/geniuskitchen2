Summer Fruit Saladüçì instructions

  1. Peel and chop apple into chunks of desired size and add to Mixing bowl..
  2. Slice strawberries and kiwi and add to bowl..
  3. Add Blueberries, Raspberries, and Blackberries to bowl..
  4. Add the juice of the 1/2 of lemon to the fruit mixture..
  5. Fold in 1/8th cup of sugar to the fruit mixture until well combined. Cover and set in fridge for 30min. (Lasts two-three days in fridge).
