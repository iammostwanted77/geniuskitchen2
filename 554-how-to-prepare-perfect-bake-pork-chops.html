Ingredients of Bake pork chops

  1. Prepare 3 of pork chops.

  6. It's 1 tbsp of of peppers.

Bake pork chops step by step

  1. In the marinated bowl mixed orange juice and ketchup mix together then add brown sugar, seasoning soy sauce and pepper mixed well..
  2. Arrange pork chops in the baking pan and pour the mixture over the pork marinated for 30 minutes..
  3. Preheated oven in 175 c and bake for 1 hour..
