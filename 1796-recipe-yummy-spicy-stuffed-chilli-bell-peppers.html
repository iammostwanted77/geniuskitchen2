Ingredients of Spicy Stuffed Chilli Bell Peppers

  1. Prepare 3 of Bell Peppers (Green, Red, Yellow).
  2. It's 250 grams of Lean Mince Meat.
  3. You need 6 slice of Pepper Jack Cheese (or something with a little kick).
  4. It's 6 tbsp of Worcestershire Sauce.
  5. It's 4 tbsp of Chilli Powder.
  6. Prepare 1 of Jalapeno.
  7. Prepare 1/4 of Onion.
  8. You need 300 grams of Rice (optional).
  9. Prepare 1 can of Red Kidney Beans.

Spicy Stuffed Chilli Bell Peppers step by step

  1. Remove center and seeds of bell peppers then wash out.
  2. Cut jalapeno & onion into small slices (Jalapeno seeds optional).
  3. Heat pan on low heat and cook meat until brown.
  4. Add 150ml water to meat stir, then add, kidney beans, onion & jalapenos. Heat for 5 mins stir well.
  5. Add chilli powder & Worcestershire sauce, stir and simmer.
  6. Preheat oven No 4.
  7. Add meat mixture from saucepan to hollowed out peppers, filling to the top. Finally placing 1 slice or grated cheese over top of each pepper.
  8. Place in oven (middle shelf) on baking tray for 5 - 10 mins.
