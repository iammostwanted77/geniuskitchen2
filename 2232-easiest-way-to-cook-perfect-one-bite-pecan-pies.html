Ingredients of One Bite Pecan Pies

  1. You need 4 oz of Philly cream cheese.
  2. It's 1/2 cup of Butter, softened.
  3. Prepare 1 cup of Flour.
  4. Prepare 1 each of Egg.
  5. Prepare 3/4 cup of Packed brown sugar.
  6. It's 1 tsp of Vanilla.
  7. Prepare 3/4 cup of Finely chopped pecans.
  8. You need 3 each of Squares, bakers semi sweet baking chocolates, melted.

One Bite Pecan Pies instructions

  1. Beat cream cheese & butter on med speed. Add flour. Mix well..
  2. Cover & refrigerate at least 1 hour.
  3. Preheat oven 375.
  4. Divide dough into 24 balls. Place 1 ball into 24 mini muffin pan.
  5. Press into bottom & sides to form a shell.
  6. Beat egg lightly. Add brown sugar & vanilla mix well. Stir in pecans.
  7. Spoon 3/4 full. Bake 25-30 min. Let stand 5 minutes..
  8. Drizzle with chocolate.
  9. I usually omit the chocolate..
