Ingredients of GOULASH - Another old Recipe

  1. It's 1 lb of lean hamburger.
  2. Prepare 1/2 of medium onion, chopped.
  3. It's 1 of Bell pepper, diced.
  4. Prepare 1 can of stewed tomatoes (15 ozs.).
  5. Prepare 1 can of tomato sauce (15 ozs.).
  6. You need 3/4 cup of water.
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp of Italian seasoning.
  8. Prepare 1 tsp of salt.
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp of black pepper.
  10. Prepare 1 tsp of Garlic powder.
  11. It's 1 tbsp of olive oil.
  12. You need 2 1/2 cup of Small elbow macaroni.

GOULASH - Another old Recipe step by step

  1. Brown burger adding onions and green bell peppers..
  2. Add seasonings, tomato sauce, stewed tomatoes and water to skillet..
  3. Let sauce simmer a minimum of one hour..
  4. Bring water to a full boil in 6 quart kettle. Add olive oil before adding macaroni..
  5. I measure the macaroni in a 2 Cup measuring cup bringing the level to the brim of the cup..
  6. Add macaroni to water in kettle when water comes to a full rolling boil. Cook for 10 minutes then drain and rinse with hot water..
  7. Put cooked macaroni back in original kettle and add sauce to the kettle and stir it in good..
  8. Serve alone or with garlic bread and/or salad as a side..
