Grandma's delicious Goulash step by step

  1. Put a pot of boiling water on the stove to make noodles. Slice the onions and bell peppers into nice sized chunks. And put aside..
  2. Get a big pot like for a stew and and brown hamburger meat in it..
  3. Now that your hamburger meat is browned. Add onions and bell pepper to the pot cover and let simmer for 15 minutes stirring occasionally..
  4. After the 15 minutes are up add in tomato soup, tomato paste, Worcestershire sauce, and ground garlic..
  5. Add water and noodles. Let simmer for 15 minutes and then let stand for 5 minutes. Bon Appetit.
