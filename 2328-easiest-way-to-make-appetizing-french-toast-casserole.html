French Toast Casserole step by step

  1. In a bowl combine milk, eggs, and vanilla..
  2. In a seperate bowl mix brown sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg..
  3. Pour and spread butter evenly in bottom of 9x13 pan..
  4. Place evenly 6 slices of bread..
  5. Pour half of milk mixture over bottom slices of bread evenly. Sprinkle half of brown sugar mixture over slices of bread..
  6. Repeat steps 4 and 5 to second layer finishing both mixtures. Cover and refrigerate overnight for next morning..
  7. Next day preheat oven at 350Â°F. Bake covered for 20 mins and uncovered for 15 mins..
  8. Serve warm with maple syrup..
