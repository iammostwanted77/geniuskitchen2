Beef goulash with mushrooms served on potato pancakes instructions

  1. Chop the beef, carrots and mushrooms..
  2. Coat the meat in flour and fry in a pan until the beef is dark..
  3. Put some water to a pot and add beef, carrots, mushrooms, bay leaves and some salt to it. Fill with water little above the ingredients..
  4. Simmer for 40 minutes, stirring every 5-8 min. After first 20 minutes add broth cubes..
  5. Cook until it's thick enough..
  6. In a meantime peel and grate the potatoes and heat a pan..
  7. Drain off any excess liquid and mix in egg, salt, and black pepper. Add enough flour to make mixture thick, about half glass all together..
  8. Fry it on a pan with oil (pancakes has to be half immersed). Turn side when the frame is golden..
  9. Fry until both sides are golden brown..
  10. Place potatoes pancakes on a plate, pour the goulash and eat!.
