Ingredients of Healthy Hummingbird Cupcakes!

  1. Prepare 162 grams of crushed pineapple.
  2. Prepare 162 grams of mashed banana.
  3. It's 1 tsp of vanilla extract.
  4. It's 2 tbsp of apple vinegar or white.
  5. Prepare 31 grams of coconut oil or veg.
  6. It's 150 head of Spelt flour.
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp of baking powder.
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp of baking soda.
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp of cinnamon.
  10. It's 65 grams of brown sugar.
  11. You need 1 tsp of stevia or more brown sugar.
  12. You need 30 grams of chopped walnuts.
  13. Prepare of a couple tablespoons of pineapple juice.

Healthy Hummingbird Cupcakes! step by step

  1. Preheat oven 176째c/350째f . In a large bowl, combine crushed pineapple, banana, vanilla extract, vinegar and coconut oil. Mix well one at a time..
  2. In another bowl mix the flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon and sugar..
  3. Mix the flour ingredients into the wet ingredients add pineapple juice as needed and fold in walnuts..
  4. Spoon in a 12 cup muffin tin and bake for 19-20 minutes (or until poked toothpick comes out clean)..
