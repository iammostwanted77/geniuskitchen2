Air Fryer Ham & Cheese Sandwiches instructions

  1. Top one side of each slice of bread with thin layer of butter. To the non buttered side add slice of colby to each followed by 2 pieces of ham to each followed by slice of provolone each..
  2. Arrange sandwiches buttered sides out in air fryer..
  3. Cook at 400 degrees for 5 minutes..
  4. Flip the sandwiches and cook another 5 minutes..
  5. They should be equally browned after a total of 5 minutes per side..
  6. Enjoy!.
