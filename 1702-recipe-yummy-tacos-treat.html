Tacos Treat instructions

  1. For Tacos Shell:Melt dark chocolate in microwave for a minute..
  2. Now add fruit essence in melted chocolate and pour it in your muffin's paper cup..
  3. Let it freeze for 2-3 minutes in refrigerator. After that separate the paper from the shell..
  4. Your Chocolate Tacos shell is ready..
  5. Icecream: Mix all the ingredients (of Icecream) simultaneously. Let it freeze in refrigerator for 5-6 hours. Your Icecream is ready!!.
  6. Tacos' Topping: Decorate your Tacos as per your preference..
  7. Here I put a layer of whipped cream, then Mango Slices, then again whipped cream, then nuts and fruits and at last put a layer of an ice-cream, mango,etc....
  8. Finally!Your Tacos Treat is ready..
