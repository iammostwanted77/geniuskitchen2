Ingredients of Creamy tomato penne

  3. Prepare 100 ml of heavy cream.

  6. Prepare 2 of green peppers.
  7. Prepare 50 g of bacon.
  8. Prepare of salt.
  9. You need of black pepper.
  10. Prepare of parmesan cheese.

Creamy tomato penne step by step

  1. Chop the garlic, cut the bacon and the vegetables..
  2. Put the penne in a pan of boiling water. Cook according to the instructions..
  3. Heat oil in a frying pan with garlic. Add bacon, then the vegetables and cook for a couple minutes..
  4. Add in a can of tomatoes into the pan. Simmer uncovered for 7-8 minutes..
  5. Reduce heat and add heavy cream. Cook for another few minutes, stirring occasionally. Add a pinch of salt..
  6. Drain the penne and mix it into the pan of tomato sauce..
  7. Serve with plenty of parmesan cheese and black pepper. Enjoy!!.
