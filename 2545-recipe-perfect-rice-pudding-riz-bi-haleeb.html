Ingredients of Rice pudding - riz bi haleeb

  1. You need 5 cups of milk.
  2. It's 1 cup of rice.
  3. It's 5 tablespoons of sugar.
  4. Prepare 1 tablespoon of cornstarch dissolved in ½ cup water.
  5. It's 1 tablespoon of rose water.
  6. Prepare 1 tablespoon of orange blossom water.
  7. It's 1/2 teaspoon of mastic gum, crushed with 1 teaspoon of sugar.

Rice pudding - riz bi haleeb instructions

  1. In a non-sticky or stainless steel pot, put the milk and rice with ½ cup water and a dash of salt. Put on medium heat and stir until the mixture thickens..
  2. While continuously stirring, add sugar and the dissolved cornstarch..
  3. After 5 min, turn the heat off; add the rose water, orange blossom water and mastic gum..
  4. Pour in serving cups and leave them to cool. Put the rice pudding in the refrigerator for a minimum of 2 hr before serving..
  5. You can serve plain or with jam next to it..
