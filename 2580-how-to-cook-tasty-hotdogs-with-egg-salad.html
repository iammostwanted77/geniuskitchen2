Hotdogs with Egg-salad step by step

  1. Wash and boil the eggs put the hotdogs in a pan with water and boil them..
  2. Peel the eggs and rinse them. Coarsely chop the eggs adding salt, cubes, mayonnaise, paprika, and dill weed..
  3. Mix the egg salad. Add to the buns and top with hotdogs..
