Ingredients of Chilli chicken

  1. It's 200 gm of boneless chicken.
  2. Prepare 2 tablespoons of corn flour.
  3. You need 1 tablespoon of rice flour.
  4. You need 1 tablespoon of wheat flour.
  5. You need 1/4 teaspoon of baking powder.
  6. You need 1 cup of water.
  7. You need 1 teaspoon of chopped garlic.
  8. You need 1 teaspoon of chopped ginger.
  9. It's 10 gm of green chillies.
  10. Prepare 10 gm of onions.
  11. You need 10 gm of capsicum.
  12. You need 1 1/2 tablespoons of light soy sauce.
  13. It's 1 1/2 tablespoons of dark soy sauce.
  14. It's 1 1/2 tablespoons of chilli paste.
  15. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon of salt.
  16. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon of sugar.
  17. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon of crushed white pepper.

Chilli chicken step by step

  1. Make a paste of corn flour, rice flour, wheat flour and baking powder using water..
  2. Dip chicken pieces in the paste and deep fry till golden brown. Keep aside..
  3. Heat oil in another pan and add the chopped garlic and ginger, followed by the diced onions, capsicum and green chilli..
  4. Now add both light and dark soy sauce, chilli paste, salt, sugar and pepper..
  5. Add the chicken and mix well. Garnish it with spring onions and parsley..
