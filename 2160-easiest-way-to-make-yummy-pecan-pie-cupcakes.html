Ingredients of Pecan Pie Cupcakes

  1. It's 1 cup of chopped pecans.
  2. You need 1/2 cup of all purpose flour.
  3. You need 1 cup of packed brown sugar.
  4. It's 2/3 cup of butter, melted.
  5. It's 1/2 tsp of cinnamon.
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp of vanilla.
