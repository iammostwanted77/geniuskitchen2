Philly cheese steak sandwiches step by step

  1. In a saucepan add olive oil with onions and bell pepper. Cook until translucent then remove from heat and sit aside..
  2. Butter bread and place on gill for 3 minutes. Set on plates and wait for meat to get done..
  3. Cook one slice of steakum to a sandwich. Cook on both sides and season with salt pepper and season salt. Then add 1/2 cup of cheese and some onions and bell peppers. Repeat steps for each sandwich..
