Cranberry Sauce instructions

  1. Grate orange peels and set aside..
  2. Peel oranges and discard membranes..
  3. Add to blender 1 bag of cranberries, 1 apple (peeled and cored), 1 orange, and 1 celery stalk..
  4. Blend until coarsely chopped; transfer to bowl..
  5. Repeat with remaining ingredients..
  6. Stir in sugar and reserved orange peel..
  7. Cover and refrigerate overnight. Very important..
