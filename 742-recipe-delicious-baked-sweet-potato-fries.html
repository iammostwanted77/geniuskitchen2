Baked sweet potato fries step by step

  1. Peel and sliced your potatoes into 1/2 " wide matchsticks..
  2. Preheat oven to 450 and liberally oil a large sheet pan with peanut oil.
  3. Toss potatoes in olive oil and season liberally.
  4. Spread out on prepared pan. Do not crowd or they will just steam..
  5. Cook on top rack 20-25 minutes turning twice throughout cook time.
  6. Allow to cool 5 minutes before serving.. it helps crispen them more.
