Bbq Chicken Four Cheese Cheesesteak Pepperoni Pizza instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Place pizza crust on a large plate.
  3. Open and drain chicken place in a bowl and chop up large pieces of chicken.
  4. Coat with bbq sauce and mix up.
  5. Fry up two slices of frozen steak slices.
  6. Dump sauce into a bowl and add all of the spices.
  7. Spread sauce onto pizza dough.
  8. Spread out a layer of cheese over sauce.
  9. Spread out bbq chicken over top layer of cheese.
  10. Spread out cooked steak.
  11. Add a layer of the cheese.
  12. Add desired amount of pepperoni.
  13. Sprinkle with some bay leaves.
  14. Place in oven directly on rack cook at 400°F for about 10 min or until crust is crispy and cheese is melted.
  15. Plate cut and enjoy!.
