Ingredients of Arroz con Leche (Mexican Rice Pudding)

  1. It's 3 cup of Whole Milk.
  2. It's 1 1/4 cup of Water.
  3. You need 1 cup of Medium grain rice.
  4. You need 2 of Cinnamon sticks.
  5. Prepare 1/4 tsp of Salt.
  6. Prepare 14 oz of Sweetened condensed milk.
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup of Raisins (soaked in warm water to soften).
  8. You need 2 tsp of Vanilla.

Arroz con Leche (Mexican Rice Pudding) step by step

  1. Bring milk, and water to a slew simmer in a large pot, over medium-low heat.
  2. Sir in rice and add cinnamon sticks. Barely simmer uncovered until rice is soft approx. 20-30 min stirring occasionally..
  3. When rice is soft, remove cinnamon sticks and stir in condensed milk, vanilla, salt and raisins..
  4. Return to a slight simmer for another 10-15 min or until most of the liquid is absorbed and rice has a pudding like consistency..
  5. Serve hot with a sprinkle of cinnamon..
