Ingredients of Sheet pan tilapia & vegetable medley

  1. You need 2 of medium Yukon gold potatoes cut into wedges.
  2. You need 3 of large fresh Brussels sprouts thinly sliced.
  3. It's 3 of large radishes thinly sliced.
  4. It's 1 cup of fresh sugar snap peas cut into 1/2 in pieces.
  5. You need 1 of small carrot thinly sliced.
  6. You need 2 tablespoons of butter melted.
  7. You need 1/2 teaspoon of garlic salt.
  8. It's 1/2 teaspoon of pepper.
  9. You need 2 of tilapia fillets 6 ounce each.
  10. You need 2 tablespoons of minced fresh tarragon or 1/2 teaspoon dried tarragon.
  11. You need 1/8 teaspoon of salt.
  12. It's 1 tablespoon of butter softened.
  13. It's of Lemon wedges and tartar sauce optional.

Great for a quick weeknight meal accompanied with fresh veggies. Plus, it's low-fat, low-cal, low-carb, KETO approved and gluten-free! This sheet pan tilapia is a delicious way to mix up your menu! It is easy, delicious, and perfect for leftovers!

Sheet pan tilapia & vegetable medley instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 line a 15x10x1 in sheet pan with foil grease foil.
  2. In a large bowl combine the first five ingredients add melted butter garlic salt and pepper toss to coat place vegetables in a single layer in prepared pan bake until potatoes are tender about 20 minutes.
  3. Remove from oven preheat broiler. Arrange vegetables on one side of sheet pan. Add fish to other side of sheet pan..
  4. Sprinkle fillets with tarragon and salt dot with softened butter broil 4-5 in from heat until the fish flakes easily with a fork about 5 minutes if desired serve with lemon wedges and tartar sauce..
