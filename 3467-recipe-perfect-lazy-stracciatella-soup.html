Ingredients of lazy stracciatella soup

  1. Prepare 1 packages of liptons chicken noodle soup.

  3. Prepare 1 bunch of frozen spinach.
  4. Prepare 1 pinch of pepper.
  5. You need 1 pinch of parmesan cheese.

lazy stracciatella soup step by step

  1. boil amount of water stated on soup package.
  2. when water boils add package of soup and frozen spinach.
  3. when soup is almost done (appox 2mins) crack an egg into the soup and stir. egg will cook in the hot broth; stiring will break up the egg..
  4. add pepper.
  5. when noodles are cooked soup is done. Pour into bowl and sprinkle parmesan cheese.
