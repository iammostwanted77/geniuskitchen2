Salisbury Steak instructions

  1. In a large bowl Mix all ingredients except for gravy mix, onions and peppers..
  2. Shape mixture into round patties..
  3. Fry the patties in a pan, with onions and peppers, flipping once, until done..
  4. While the patties are cooking. Cook the gravy in a separate pot. Follow the package directions..
  5. As the patties r done, remove from pan. Drain grease and clean the pan. Return patties to pan and top with the gravy, onions, and peppers..
  6. Enjoy :).
