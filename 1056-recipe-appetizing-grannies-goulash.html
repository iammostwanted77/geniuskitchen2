grannies goulash instructions

  1. coat meat in cornflour.
  2. heat oil in pan.
  3. add meat and brown.
  4. add sliced onion and crushed garlic till onions soften add beef and brown.
  5. add beef stock and wine bring to boil add carrots.
  6. put the tomato puree and paprika in.
  7. leave to simmer for couple of hours add more stock and seasoning if needed.
  8. for rice..wash rice and place in pan with salt cover with water bring to boil.
  9. slice spring onion set aside fry garlic till soft mix both through cooked rice and serve with goulash.
