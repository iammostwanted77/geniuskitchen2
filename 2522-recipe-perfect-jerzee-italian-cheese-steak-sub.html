Jerzee Italian Cheese Steak Sub instructions

  1. break up frozen steaks into small pieces.
  2. chop onions and peppers finely.
  3. heat skillet to frying temp, toss in broken sandwich steaks onion and peppers.
  4. bake or deep fry fries/set aside/continue to next step.
  5. continue to fry until meat is brown and onions are limp.
  6. add steak sauce and olive oil stirring occasionally.
  7. make sure all ingredients are distributed equally in pan. Cover and let simmer on low heat for about 3 to 4 mins.
  8. split open rolls and spread with cheese sauce on both sides.
  9. top rolls with steak mixture, than top that with roughly a hand full of fries, smash closed cut in halves(if desired) enjoy!!.
