Beef chili dry with fried rice step by step

  1. Marinate beef with salt,black pepper,white pepper soya sauce vinegar corn flour maida and tenderizer.
  2. Boil the rice with salt.
  3. In oil fry garlic add carrots, cabbage and capsicum add salt,black pepper white pepper soya sauce chilli sauce chicken powder add boiled rice toss well..
  4. Fry the marinated beef on high flame for 2 minutes then lower the flameand cook until done.(add little water mix with corn flour for gravy if u want).
  5. Finally add green chillies onions and spring onions..
  6. Serve hot with rice.
