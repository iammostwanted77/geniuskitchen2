Ingredients of Moussaka

  1. Prepare 1 tbsp of olive oil & spray.
  2. You need 1 of onion - finely chopped.
  3. It's 1 clove of garlic.
  4. Prepare 500 grams of minced lamb.
  5. You need 500 grams of jar vegetable pasta sauce.
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup of parsley.
  7. It's 2 of eggs.
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup of grated parmesan.
  9. You need 2 tsp of dried oregano.
  10. Prepare 1/2 tsp of cinnamon.
  11. It's 1 of eggplant - cut 1cm thick.
  12. It's 2 large of potatoes - cooked.
  13. It's 250 ml of Philadelphia original cream for cooking.

Moussaka instructions

  1. Saute onion & garlic 2 minutes, add mince and brown well. Add sauce, parsley, oregano & cinnamon. Simmer 10 minutes..
  2. Boil potato, chop, and grill eggplant with oil..
  3. Layer eggplant, potato and meat..
  4. Whisk Phillips, eggs & parmesan and pour over. Bake 180° for 20- 30 minutes..
