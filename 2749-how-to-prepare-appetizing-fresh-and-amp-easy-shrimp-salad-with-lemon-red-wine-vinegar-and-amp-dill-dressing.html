Ingredients of Fresh & Easy Shrimp Salad with Lemon, Red Wine Vinegar, & Dill Dressing

  1. Prepare 1 cup of medium precooked shrimp cocktail type shrimp.
  2. It's 1 cup of English (Hot House) cucumber that's been seeded and cut into 1/4" cubes.
  3. Prepare 1 cup of sweet bell pepper (red, orange, or yellow are good) cut into 1/4" pieces.
  4. It's 1 cup of cherry or grape tomatoes that have been cut in half.
  5. It's 2/3 cup of sweet or red onion cut into 1/4" pieces.
  6. It's 3 Tablespoons of chopped fresh dill or 1 Tablespoon dry dill.
  7. It's 1 Tablespoon of fresh lemon juice.
  8. You need 2 Tablespoons of Red Wine Vinegar.
  9. It's 3/4-1 teaspoon of salt.
  10. It's 1 teaspoon of sugar.
  11. Prepare 1 Tablespoon of olive oil.
