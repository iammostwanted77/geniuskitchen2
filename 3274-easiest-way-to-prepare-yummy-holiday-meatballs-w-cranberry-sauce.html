Holiday Meatballs w/Cranberry Sauce instructions

  1. Combine dry ingredients in a medium bowl..
  2. Combine wet ingredients in a medium bowl..
  3. Mix wet ingredients with ground beef..
  4. Mix dry ingredients with ground beef..
  5. Form meatballs into bite size pieces (makes 40-45 meatballs)..
  6. Place on baking sheet in rows leaving a small space between each meatball..
  7. Bake on 400Â°F for 20 mins..
  8. While the meatballs cook you can prepare the cranberry sauce.
  9. In a medium sized saucepan over medium heat, dissolve the sugar in the orange juice..
  10. Stir in the cranberries and cook until the cranberries start to pop (about 10 minutes)..
  11. Remove from heat and place sauce in a large serving bowl. Cranberry sauce will thicken as it cools.
  12. When meatballs finish cooking, drain any excess grease and add meatballs to the cranberry sauce..
  13. Cover meatballs completly w/cranberry sauce and serve..
