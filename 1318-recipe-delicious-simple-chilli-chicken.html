Ingredients of Simple chilli chicken

  3. It's 3 tsp of corn flour.
  4. Prepare to taste of Salt and sugar according.
  5. It's 1 tsp of red chilli powder.
  6. You need 1 of chopped onion and green chilli.
  7. It's 1/2 cup of chopped capsicum.
  8. You need 3 tsp of oil.
  9. You need 1 tsp of soy sauce,vinegar,tomato sauce.
  10. You need 1 tsp of chilli sauce..
  11. Prepare 2 clove of chopped garlic.
  12. It's 1tsp of chopped ginger.

Simple chilli chicken instructions
