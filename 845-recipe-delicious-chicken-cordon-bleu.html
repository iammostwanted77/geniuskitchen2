Ingredients of Chicken cordon bleu

  1. You need 8 slice of deli ham.
  2. You need 4 slice of swiss cheese or your preference.
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp of all-purpose flour.
  4. Prepare 1 of egg beaten.
  5. It's 1 cup of panko bread crumb.
  6. Prepare 1 of salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Prepare 4 of boneless chicken cut or pound thin.

Chicken cordon bleu instructions

  1. Pre heat oven to 450.
  2. Season the breast..
  3. Lay the ham and cheese on the breast and roll them tight..
  4. Put each in its own bowl. Then take the breast and go flour, to egg, to bread crumb..
  5. Put on a baking dish and bake for 15 to 18 minutes. Or till firm to touch and have bread crumb brown..
