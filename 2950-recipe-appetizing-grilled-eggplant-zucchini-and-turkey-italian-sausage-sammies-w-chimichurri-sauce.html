Grilled Eggplant, Zucchini, and Turkey Italian Sausage Sammies w/ Chimichurri Sauce instructions

  1. Warm up grill and preheat over to 425..
  2. Place head of garlic in tin foil with a tbsp of water, seal and place on grill and turn frequently.
  3. Slice eggplant and zuchinni.
  4. Mix 1/2 cup of EVOO, garlic and cilantro.
  5. Spread butter on brat buns, add garlic powder, parmesan and paprika.
  6. Brush Eggplant and Zucchini with EVOO and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  7. Grill Eggplant, zucchini and turkey sausage.
  8. Place buns in oven and cook until there is a slight crisp to them especially the inside..
  9. Squeeze garlic out of jackets, smash and add pinch of salt.
  10. Assembly: Spread garlic paste onto buns, squirt of spicy brown mustard, place 2 slices of eggplant, 3-4 slices of zucchini, sausage and a liberal tbsp of chimichurri sauce..
