Ingredients of Slow Cooker Salisbury Steak

  1. You need 2 lbs of lean ground beef.
  2. You need 1 envelope (1 ounce) of dry onion soup mix.
  3. It's 1/2 cup of Italian seasoned bread crumbs.
  4. It's 1/4 cup of milk.
  5. You need 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour.
  6. Prepare 2 tbs of vegetable oil.
  7. Prepare 2 cans (10.75 ounces) of condensed cream of chicken soup.
  8. It's 1 packet (1 ounce) of dry au jus mix.
  9. It's 3/4 cup of water.

Slow Cooker Salisbury Steak instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the ground beef, onion soup mix, bread crumbs, and milk using your hands. Shape into 8 patties..
  2. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Dredge the patties in flour dry to coat, and quickly brown on both sides in the hot skillet..
  3. Place browned patties into the slow cooker, stacking alternately, like a pyramid..
  4. In a medium bowl, mix together the cream of chicken soup, au jus mix, and water. Pour over the meat. Cook on the Low setting for 4-5 hours, until ground beef is well done..
