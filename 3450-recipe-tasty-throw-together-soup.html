throw together soup instructions

  1. take the "insides" out of chicken and rinse.
  2. boil chicken in large pot.
  3. add all herbs whatever kind you like and all seasonings to taste.
  4. add butter and let it boil untill chicken is cooked through... note some water will evaporate so I just kept adding water as it boiled down.
  5. once chicken is cooked let cool and shred make sure to get all the bones out.
  6. boil noodles, drain noodles and canned vegetables throw in pot with broth and chicken. I let mine simmer a bit to bring all the flavors together.
  7. enjoy. like I said it's completely versatile you can use whatever you like... I didn't even have noodles I just took 2 boxes of macaroni and just cooked the noodles would've been great with canned tomatoes too....
