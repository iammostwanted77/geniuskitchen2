Ingredients of Pecan Pie (deep dish)

  1. Prepare 1 cup of pecan halves.
  2. You need 3 large of eggs.
  3. It's 1 cup of corn syrup.
  4. You need 3/4 cup of sugar.
  5. It's 1/2 stick of of butter(melted).
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp of vanilla.
  7. Prepare 1 of unbaked deep dish pie crust.

Pecan Pie (deep dish) step by step

  1. preheat oven 350.
  2. pour cup of pecans in pie crust.
  3. in bowl mix sugar, corn syrup, butter and vanilla.
  4. in another bowl wisp eggs.
  5. then combine the bowls and mix.
  6. pour over pecans in pie crust.
  7. bake for 55min.
