Ingredients of Chocolate-Bourbon Pecan Pie

  1. It's 1 each of 9" Deep Dish Pie Crust.

  7. It's 1 tbsp of Flour.
  8. You need 4 each of Eggs.
  9. Prepare 1/4 lb of Butter, Unsalted (1 Stick).
  10. You need 1/2 cup of Semisweet (or bittersweet) chocolate chips.

Chocolate-Bourbon Pecan Pie step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Gently melt butter on stove top or in a microwave. When the butter is melted, but just before it simmers, add chocolate chips and stir until fully melted..
  3. Put pecans into pie crust and set aside..
  4. Combine sugars, corn syrup, bourbon, and flour in a bowl and combine thoroughly..
  5. Whisk in eggs, two at a time, untill fully incorporated..
  6. Slowly whisk in melted butter and chocolate until completely emulsified..
  7. Pour liquid mixture into pie shell..
  8. Bake the pie for 1 hr and 10 min, or until completely set in the middle..
  9. Allow to cool completely before slicing..
