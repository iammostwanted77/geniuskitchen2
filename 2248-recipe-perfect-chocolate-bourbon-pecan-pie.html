Ingredients of Chocolate-bourbon-pecan pie

  3. It's 1 cup of Light corn syrup.
  4. It's 6 tbsp of Butter,melted.
  5. It's 1/2 cup of Sugar.
  6. It's 1/4 cup of Firmly packed light brown sugar.
  7. It's 3 tbsp of Bourbon.
  8. You need 1 tbsp of All-purpose flour.
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp of Vanilla extract.
  10. Prepare 1 cup of Coarsely chopped pecans.

Chocolate-bourbon-pecan pie step by step

  1. Fit piecrust into a 9-inch pie plate according to package directions. fold edges under, and crimp.
  2. Whisk together eggs and next 7 ingredients until thoroughly blended. Stir in pecans and morsels. Pour pie mixture into piecrust; place pie on a baking sheet..
  3. Bake at 350° on lowest oven rack for 55 mins our until set. Cool on a wire rack 1 hour our until completely cool..
