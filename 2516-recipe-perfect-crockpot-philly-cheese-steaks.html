Ingredients of Crockpot Philly Cheese Steaks

  1. You need of cheese steaks.
  2. You need 2 lb of round steak, sliced.
  3. Prepare 2 of green bell peppers, sliced.
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp of butter, melted.
  5. You need 1 tsp of salt.
  6. You need 1/2 cup of water.
  7. Prepare 2 tsp of beef bouillon granules.
  8. Prepare 8 of hoagie rolls, split in half.
  9. You need 8 slice of provolone cheese, cut in half.

Crockpot Philly Cheese Steaks instructions

  2. Whisk together water and bouillon in small bowl.
  3. Pour into crockpot, cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours..
  4. Remove beef, onions, and bell peppers from crockpot and divide evenly between 8 rolls. Top with cheese and place under boiler until cheese is melted..
