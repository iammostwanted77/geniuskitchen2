Ingredients of Cranberry sauce

  1. It's 12 oz of fresh cranberries.
  2. You need 1 cup of Demerara sugar or coarse unbleached sugar.
  3. It's 4 oz of cranberry juice blend.
  4. You need 1/2 tsp of salt.
  5. You need 1 cup of agar agar loosely cut.
  6. You need 1 cup of water.
  7. Prepare 1 tsp of baking soda.

Cranberry sauce instructions

  1. In a pot add the cranberries add juice and baking soda.
  2. Add salt let cook till berries brust about 15 minutes.
  3. Cut the agar agar into pieces add to berries keep boiling till mostly desolved strain in a sieve..
  4. Pour into a form you could pour into molds for individual servings I put ice water around the plastic form so it wouldn't melt.
