Ingredients of Beef Pot Roast

  1. You need 3 lb of beef roast - seasoned and seared.
  2. You need 1 1/2 lb of red potatoes - quartered.
  3. Prepare 1 lb of baby carrots.
  4. Prepare 1 lb of white mushrooms - halved.
  5. It's 1 bunch of celery - last 1/3 - 1/2 of leafy ends of whole bunch.
  6. Prepare 1 large of onion - quartered.
  7. It's 1 head of garlic - sliced in half horizontally.
  8. It's 2 can of beef broth.
  9. You need 1 of (seasoned) salt, pepper, garlic/onion powder... any favorite spices.

Beef Pot Roast step by step

  1. turn crock pot on high - add broth (you can also use dutch oven - preheat oven to 300* add all ingredients to pot & cook 4-6 hours - until meat is tender).
  2. season meat with salt, pepper, etc. - seer in skillet & lay in broth (you can deglaze pan with red wine or sherry then add to pot).
  3. add all other ingredients & any desired spices.
  4. cook on high about 6-7 hours - or on low 8-9 hours (until meat is tender) - making sure there is plenty of juice (just add water).
