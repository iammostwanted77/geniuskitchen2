Ingredients of Meat Loaf

  1. Prepare 2 lb of Ground Turkey.
  2. You need 1 packages of Saltines.
  3. It's 1/2 cup of Milk.
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup of Minced Onions.
  5. It's 2 each of Eggs.
  6. It's 2 tsp of Salt.
  7. You need 1/4 tsp of Pepper.
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp of Worchestershire Sauce.
  9. You need 2 tbsp of Soy Sauce.
  10. It's 1 tsp of Dry Mustard.
  11. You need 2 tbsp of Catchup.
  12. It's 1/2 tsp of Oregano.

Meat Loaf instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 * degrees.
  2. mix turkey meat with milk, eggs, onion..
  3. Put tube of crackers, dry mustard, salt, pepper, oregano in blender and blend into crumbs.
  4. Mix in catchup, soy sauce, worchestershire sauce..
  5. Mix all by hand until blended well then place meat into loaf pan and place upside down is large casarol dish.
  6. Place in oven and bake for 30 min. remove from oven and remove loaf pan..
  7. Place back in oven and bake another 65 min..
  8. When done remove and let stand 25 min. before cutting..
