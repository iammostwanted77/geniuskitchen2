Instant biscuit Modak instructions

  2. Add condensed milk in parle g biscuit and add 1/2 of the dryfruit powder and coconut..
  3. Mix well and make a modak in modak mould and keep aside..

  5. Add coco powder, chocolate powder and dry coconut and mix well..

  7. Take a small ball from the dough and make modak..
