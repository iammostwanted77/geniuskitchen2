Grandma Yetta's Chicken Soup FUSF instructions

  1. Remove skin from chicken.
  2. Peel 2 carrots and parsnips, cut lengthwise..
  3. Cut onion in half.
  4. Chop celery into large pieces.
  5. Add all ingredients with salt to a large pot and cover generously with water..
  6. Bring to a boil, then turn to a simmer for 45 minutes, sirring occasionally..
  7. Strain broth and return to pot..
  8. Reserve vegetables for future use.
  9. Once chicken has cooled enough, debone and shred..
  10. Peel and cut remaining carrot into small rounds.
  11. Place chicken and carrot into broth..
  12. Simmer for 5-10 minutes until carrot has just softened. Serve with egg noodles or matzoh balls.
  13. Matzoh directions:.
  14. Beat the eggs lightly.
  15. Add wet ingredients and salt..
  16. Thoroughly mix with matzoh meal..
  17. Refriegerate for 30 minutes.
  18. Partially fill a large pot with water and bring to a boil..
  19. Form balls about 1" in diameter. drop into boiling water, reduce to a simmer. Cook for 20-30 minutes..
