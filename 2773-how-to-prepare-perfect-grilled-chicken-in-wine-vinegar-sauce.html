Grilled chicken in wine vinegar sauce instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken with pinch of salt,fresh lemon juice and 2 tablespoon red wine vinegar for 2hours..
  2. Set the microwave oven at 210°C .Then grill the chicken for 15 minutes..
  3. Heat oil in a vessel.Then fry onion and garlic unitll they turns golden brown..
  4. Add grilled chicken into the vessel.cook for 3 minutes.Stir continuously..
  5. Do the flame low now.Add orange juice to it.then put cornflour and some water in the vessel and bring it to boil for 3 minutes..
  6. Add tomato,nuts,cinnamon,coriander,cardamom,dry chillies to it..
  7. Cook for 5minutes in high flame.stir occassionally.Then add 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar to it..
  8. Add tomato souce,rose water,red pepper sauce and oregano to it.Then cook it for 5 minutes continuously in medium flame and stir often..
  9. Now the dish is ready to eat.Serve it hot with rice or bread.Enjoy the sour taste of the gravy..
