Ingredients of Moms Pecan Pies

  1. You need 5 of eggs.
  2. You need 1 1/2 of sugar.
  3. Prepare 2 cup of Karo light syrup.
  4. You need 3 tsp of vanilla.
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp of butter.
  6. It's 2 tsp of flour.
  7. You need 2 cup of pecan halves or pieces.

Moms Pecan Pies step by step

  1. preheat oven to 350.
  2. Beat eggs lightly.
  3. add all ingredients.
  4. stir in pecans.
  5. bake for 55 minutes.
  6. makes 2 pies.
