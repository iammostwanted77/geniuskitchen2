Carrot Laced Soup step by step

  1. Wash, peel and cut small carrot flowers. Insert these flowers in a raw noodles carefully. Boil them in big pot for 7 minutes..
  2. Boil chicken with five glasses of water for 15-20 minutes..
  3. Separate chicken from bones and shredded it..
  4. Add salt, chicken powder, black pepper, vinegar, soya sauce and hot sauce in chicken broth and let it cook..
  5. Finally beat eggs in clean dry bowl and pour in soup pot in circle. Don't mix till 10 seconds..
  6. Now gradually mix with wooden spoon..
  7. Pour lace carrot noodles on soup bowls.
  8. Serve hot with crackers. You can adjust cornflour and spices to you likeness..
