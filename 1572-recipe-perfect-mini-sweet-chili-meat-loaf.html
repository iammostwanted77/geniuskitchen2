Mini sweet chili meat loaf step by step

  1. Season ground turkey with cajun seasoning salt pepper and pinch of onion powder.
  2. Next add in egg,bread crumbs and bell peppers.
  3. Mix ingredients together by hand until everything is evenly mixed..
  4. Form mini meat loafs on a non stick cooking pan bake at 350 degrees for 45 mins or until desired cook time.
  5. Coat with sweet chili sauce every 15 mins until done.
  6. Enjoy !.
