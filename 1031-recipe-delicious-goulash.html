Ingredients of Goulash

  1. You need 1 lb of ground beef.
  2. You need 1 of onion, chopped.
  3. Prepare 1 of green pepper, chopped.
  4. Prepare 1 box of elbow macaroni.
  5. It's 6 clove of minced garlic, separated.
  6. You need 2 tsp of dried oregano, separated.
  7. Prepare 1 tsp of dried basil, separated.
  8. You need 1 can of 28oz tomato sauce.
  9. Prepare 1 can of 14.5 oz diced tomatoes.
  10. You need 1 can of 6oz tomato paste.
  11. Prepare 1 tsp of garlic powder.
  12. You need 1 tsp of Mrs dash table blend, or 1/2 tsp salt.
  13. Prepare 1/2 tsp of black pepper.
  14. Prepare 1 pinch of sugar.

Goulash instructions

  1. saute onions, green peppers and 4 cloves garlic until soft, about 2 mins.
  2. add ground beef, 2 cloves of garlic, 1 tsp oregano, 1/2 tsp basil and cook until browned.
  3. stir in tomato sauce, paste, and sauce.
  4. add garlic powder, Mrs dash, pepper, and remaining oregano and basil.
  5. cover and summer for 10 minutes.
  6. meanwhile, cook elbow macaroni as directed.
  7. Mix meat sauce and noodles together.
  8. add pinch of sugar before serving.
