Ingredients of Eggplant Rollatini

  1. It's 1 of eggplant, peeled and cut long ways.
  2. You need 2-3 cups of breadcrumbs.
  3. You need 3 of eggs, beaten.
  4. It's 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese.
  5. It's 15 oz of container of ricotta cheese.
  6. You need 3 cloves of garlic, chopped.
  7. You need 1 lb of mozzarella- slice about 2/3s and grate the rest so it’s shredded in pieces (about 2 tbsp of shredded cheese).
  8. It's 2 cups of tomato sauce.
  9. You need of Frying oil, enough to cover about 1 inch of your pan.
  10. It's of Dried parsley.
  11. It's of Salt, pepper.
  12. You need of Dried basil.
  13. Prepare of Toothpicks.

Eggplant Rollatini step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 350..
  2. Season eggs with salt and pepper. Mix breadcrumbs and Parmesan cheese..
  3. Dredge eggplant in egg, then breadcrumb mixture..
  4. Fry til golden brown.
  5. In the meantime mix ricotta with the shredded mozzarella, chopped garlic, salt, pepper, parsley and basil..
  6. Scoop spoonfuls of ricotta mixture into the middle of the eggplant..
  7. Roll up and secure with toothpicks..
  8. Place rolled up eggplant in baking dish. Once its placed remove the toothpicks..
  9. Spread sauce evenly and top with the sliced mozzarella..
  10. Bake for 30 minutes or til cheese is golden bubbly..
  11. Serve with garlic bread or pasta..
