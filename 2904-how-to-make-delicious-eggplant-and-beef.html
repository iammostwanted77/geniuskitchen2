Ingredients of Eggplant and beef

  1. You need 1/3 lb of eye of round beef.
  2. It's 7 oz of tomato sauce.
  3. It's 1 tsp of whole leaf oregano.
  4. Prepare 1/4 tsp of salt.
  5. You need 1/4 cup of chopped parsley.
  6. You need 1/4 tsp of ground black pepper.
  7. Prepare 1/3 tsp of onion powder.
  8. Prepare 1/3 tsp of granulated garlic powder.
  9. It's 1 1/2 cup of cubed eggplant.
  10. It's 1/3 cup of grated parmesan cheese.
  11. It's 2 tbsp of butter.
  12. You need 1/2 cup of sliced olives and pimentos.

Eggplant and beef instructions

  1. Slice the beef into ribbons and thinly.
  2. Add butter to a pan heat. Add the beef and eggplant. Add salt, pepper, garlic, and onion powder. Stir well.
  3. Simmer 5 minutes. Add sauce, oregano, olives, and parsley. Let simmer stirring occasionally. When eggplant is done add cheese on top cover 5 minutes then serve, hope you enjoy!.
