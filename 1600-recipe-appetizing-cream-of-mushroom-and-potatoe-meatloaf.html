cream of mushroom and potatoe meatloaf instructions

  1. ptehead oven to 400°F.
  2. in large bowl combine meat, onion, green pepper, salt, pepper, minced garlic, BBQ, ketchup,bread crumbs.
  3. form mixture into a large laof and transfure to baking dish for stews.
  4. add to baking pan chopped potatoes and carrots.
  5. top carrots and potatoes with cream of mushroom soup.
  6. add more salt and pepper if desired.
  7. top meat loaf with brown sugar and a squeeze of ketchup on top.
  8. pop in oven for spprox. 1 to 1 1/2 hrs. until meat is cooked threw and potatoes are tender! enjoy :).
  9. allow to cool.
