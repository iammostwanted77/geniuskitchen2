Ingredients of Lightly-flavored Chinese Soup with Cellophane Noodles and Chicken Skin

  1. You need 2 of Chicken skin.
  2. Prepare 5 of to 6 stocks Chinese chives.
  3. It's 1/2 small of bunch Enoki mushrooms.
  4. It's 90 grams of Cellophane noodles.
  5. It's 2 of Eggs.
  6. It's 500 ml of Water.
  7. Prepare 1 tsp of ★ Salt.
  8. You need 2 tsp of ★ Soy sauce.
  9. You need 2 tsp of Chinese soup stock.
  10. It's 1 of White sesame seeds.
  11. It's 1 of Ra-yu.

Lightly-flavored Chinese Soup with Cellophane Noodles and Chicken Skin instructions

  1. Cut the chicken skin into bite-sizes. Chop up the Chinese chives into 2 cm-sized pieces. Cut the enoki mushrooms into thirds. Cook the cellophane noodles..
  2. Boil the water in a pot. Add the Chinese soup stock and chicken skin. Simmer for 3 minutes. Then add the vegetables and the cellophane noodles. Season with ★ condiments..
  3. Turn the heat up high. Beat the eggs and drizzle it in the soup. Sprinkle the white sesame seeds to finish. Dish it up, and serve with ra-yu if you'd like..
