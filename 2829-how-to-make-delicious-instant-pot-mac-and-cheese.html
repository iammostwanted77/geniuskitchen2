Ingredients of Instant Pot Mac and Cheese

  1. You need 2 1/2 cups of dry elbow macaroni.
  2. You need 3 cups of water.
  3. It's 1/2 tsp of onion powder.
  4. Prepare 1 tsp of dry mustard.
  5. You need 1/4 tsp of seasoned salt.
  6. It's 1/4 tsp of pepper.
  7. You need 1 tbls of butter.
  8. You need 3 oz of cream cheese.
  9. Prepare 3/4 cup of milk divided.
  10. You need 2 1/2 cup of shredded cheddar cheese.
  11. It's 2 tbls of shredded parmesan cheese.

Instant Pot Mac and Cheese step by step

  1. Add macaroni, water, seasonings and butter to Instant Pot..
  2. Set timer for 6 minutes on high pressure..
  3. Release pressure and leave water, add in cream cheese until melted..
  4. Add in 1/4 cup milk and cheeses. Stir until smooth, while adding rest of milk. You may not need all milk..
