Ingredients of Ginger Garlic Pot Roast

  1. You need 4 lb of beef round roast.
  2. Prepare 1/3 cup of sherry.
  3. Prepare 1 1/2 oz of ginger puree.
  4. You need 2/3 cup of soy sauce.
  5. You need 1 1/2 tsp of garlic powder.
  6. Prepare 16 oz of frozen broccoli.
  7. You need 6 oz of water chestnuts.

Ginger Garlic Pot Roast step by step

  1. season roast with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and ginger powder.
  2. brown roast in skillet..
  3. transfer roast to crockpot.
  4. mix soy sauce, sherry, ginger puree, and garlic powder..
  5. pour mixture over roast..
  6. crock pot set for 7 hrs.
  7. add broccoli and water chestnuts 30 minutes before serving..
  8. serve with steamed rice..
