Ingredients of Rice Pudding (Arroz con Leche)

  1. Prepare 7 cup of water.
  2. You need 1 cup of uncooked long grain rice.
  3. You need 2 large of cinnamon sticks.
  4. You need 5 of cloves.
  5. You need 1 can of evaporated milk.
  6. You need 1 can of sweetened condensed milk.
  7. Prepare 1 cup of whole milk.
  8. It's 1/2 cup of golden raisins.

Rice Pudding (Arroz con Leche) step by step

  1. Add water, cinnamon sticks and cloves in large simmering pot. Bring to boil..
  2. Once water is rapidly boiling add rice and stir. Lower heat to medium flame. Cook uncovered for about 25 min..
  3. Remove all cloves and both cinnamon sticks. Drain rice in a mesh strainer. Place rice back in pan..
  4. Lower heat to low. Add whole milk, then stir. Add both the evaporated and sweetened milks. Continue stirring constantly until liquid thickens. Make sure to scrape bottom of pan so the rice or liquid doesn't stick. About 15 min..
  5. Turn off stove top. Add raisins and stir. They'll plump up with the heat from the cooked rice. Let pudding sit for about 20 minutes and serve. Enjoy!! Pudding could be eaten warm or cold. Your choice..
  6. Be sure to refrigerate any remaining leftovers..
