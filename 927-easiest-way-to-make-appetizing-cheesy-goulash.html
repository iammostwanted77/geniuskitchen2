Ingredients of Cheesy Goulash

  1. It's 1 lb. of hamburger.
  2. It's 1 of small onion, chopped.
  3. You need 1 (14.5 oz) of can petite diced tomatoes.
  4. Prepare 1 (15 oz) of can tomato sauce.
  5. Prepare 2 cups of uncooked elbow macaroni.
  6. Prepare 1 cup of beef broth.
  7. You need 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese.
  8. It's of Seasonings to taste: pepper, garlic powder, Italian sg, parsley.

Cheesy Goulash step by step

  1. In a large pot cook onion, hamburger and seasonings until browned..
  2. Drain and return to pot..
  3. Add in tomatoes, tomato sauce, macaroni & beef broth..
  4. Bring to a boil; reduce heat to medium low and simmer covered for about 20 minutes..
  5. Cook until moisture is absorbed & macaroni is cooked. Add in 1/2 cup cheddar cheese; stir to combine..
  6. Rurn off heat & top with remaining cheese..
  7. Place lid back on just long enough to melt cheese..
  8. Enjoy!.
