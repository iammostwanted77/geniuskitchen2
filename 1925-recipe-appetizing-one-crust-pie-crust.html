One-Crust Pie Crust step by step

  1. In medium bowl, combine flour and salt..
  2. Using pastry blender or fork, cut shortening into flour until mixture resembles coarse crumbs..
  3. Sprinkle flour mixture with water 1 tablespoon at a time, while tossing and mixing lightly with fork. Add water until dough is just moist enough to form a ball when lightly pressed together. (Too much water causes dough to become sticky and tough; too little water causes edges to crack and pastry to tear easily while rolling.).
  4. Shape dough into ball..
  5. Flatten ball to 1/2 inch thickness, rounding and smoothing edges..
  6. On floured surface, roll lightly from center to edge into 11 inch circle..
  7. Fold pastry in half; place in 9 inch pie pan or 9 or 10 inch tart pan. Unfold; gently press in bottom and up sides of pan. Do not stretch..
  8. If using pie pan, fold edge under to form a standing rim; flute edges. If using tart pan, trim pastry edges of necessary..
  9. For filled one crust pie: Fill and bake as directed in recipe..
  10. For baked pie shell (unfilled): Prick bottom and sides of pastry generously with fork. Bake at 450 degrees for 9 to 12 minutes or until light golden brown; cool. Continue as directed in recipe..
