Ingredients of Pumpkin Pie Rice Pudding

  1. Prepare 1 1/2 cup of uncooked minute rice.
  2. You need 1 can of 12 oz evaporated milk.
  3. You need 1 cup of canned pumpkin.
  4. Prepare 1 cup of water.
  5. You need 1/2 cup of packed dark brown sugar.
  6. You need 2 tsp of pumpkin pie spice.
  7. It's 1/4 tsp of salt.
  8. You need 1 tsp of vanilla extract.

This vegan & gluten free recipe is great for Christmas & thanksgiving. COMBINE rice, evaporated milk, pumpkin, water, sugar, pumpkin pie spice and salt in medium saucepan. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla extract. Pudding will thicken as it sits.

Pumpkin Pie Rice Pudding step by step

  1. Combine rice, evaporated milk, pumpkin, water, brown sugar, pumpkin pie spice and salt in a medium saucepan..
  2. Cook over medium heat 20 minutes, stirring frequently. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla extract. Let stand for 5-10 minutes before serving..
  3. Garnish with a dusting of pumpkin pie spice, whipped cream or crushed pecans if desired..

Pumpkin Pie Rice Pudding: The Rice. Rice is the most important ingredient in rice pudding. It affects both the texture and flavor of the final pudding. For a classic textured pudding, use long-grain white rice. If you'd prefer a creamy, risotto-like rice pudding, use a medium or short-grain rice.
