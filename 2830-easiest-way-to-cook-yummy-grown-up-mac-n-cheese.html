Ingredients of Grown Up Mac n Cheese

  1. It's 1 stick of butter.
  2. You need 1/4 Cup of milk.
  3. Prepare 1/4 Cup of grated parmesan.
  4. Prepare 1/4 Cup of shredded mozzarella.
  5. It's 1/4 Cup of white cheddar shredded.
  6. Prepare Dash of black pepper.
  7. It's of Fresh basil.

Grown Up Mac n Cheese step by step

  1. Melt butter and add milk.
  2. Once milk is hot slowly stir in cheddar, then mozzarella, then the parmesan. Cook on medium low until fully melted and incorporated..
  3. Pour sauce over preferred pasta. Garnish with pepper and fresh chopped basil.
