Ingredients of white chicken chilli

  1. It's 1 packages of McCormick White Chilli Seasoning.
  2. It's 1 lb of chicken breast; cubed.
  3. Prepare 2 can of cannellini beans; 1 can drained.
  4. It's 1 can of sweet corn; drained.
  5. It's 2 can of rotel; diced tomatoes & green chillies.
  6. You need 1 cup of shredded cheese; mozzerella/provolone.
  7. You need 1/4 of onion; chopped.
  8. Prepare 1/4 of sour cream.
  9. You need 2 tbsp of Morton's "Natures Seasons" Seasoning Blend.

white chicken chilli step by step

  1. In a large skillet, season and cook cubed chicken until outside is white. Do Not Drain. (I use Natures seasons to season all my foods. it is a mixture of celery, parsley, garlic, salt, pepper, and onion. Very light seasonings blend, but the flavor pops! Been using it for 15 years).
  2. Whisk together chilli seasoning and 1cup cold water; pour mixture over chicken..
  3. Add both cans of beans; one drained and one not, along with corn, onion, and rotel. Simmer 15 minutes, covered, stirring occasionally..
  4. Remove from heat, stir in sour cream and shredded cheese. Add Natures Seasons, mix well and serve with your choice of crackers or chips. My family likes the Tostitos Catalina tortilla chips..
  5. Enjoy.
