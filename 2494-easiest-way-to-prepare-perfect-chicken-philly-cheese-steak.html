Ingredients of Chicken philly cheese steak

  1. It's 1/2 cup of mushroom (cut finely).
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup of sweet onions.
  3. It's 1/2 cup of green pepper (thinly sliced).
  4. You need 1 tbsp of olive oil.
  5. You need 1 tbsp of season salt, black pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, and worchestershire sauce.
  6. It's 1 packages of boneless skinless chicken breast.
  7. You need 1 packages of french hoagie buns.
  8. It's 1/4 tsp of barbecue sauce.
  9. You need 1/4 tsp of miracle whip.
  10. You need 2 slice of provolone cheese.
  11. You need 2 slice of white American cheese.

Chicken philly cheese steak instructions

  1. Before you start cooking make sure to chop your onion, green pepper and mushrooms..
  2. Then chop your chicken breast in small cubes. And add your seasonings. Put to the side.
  3. In a non stick pan add your oil to sautee your veggies. Cook until their translucent..
  4. Once you've done that remove your veggies and put to the side. In the same pan add your chicken. Cook until chicken is fully cooked..
  5. Once your chicken is done add your veggies back in the pan. Make sure the chicken and veggies are well mixed.
  6. Now get your provolone and white american cheese and add that on top of the chicken. Lower heat and cover for a minute so cheese can melt..
  7. Get a hoagie roll and spoon miracle whip and barbecue sauce on. I put my buns in the over for minute before adding the condiments. But the choice is yours..
  8. Add the meat and you're ready to eat. Note: you can also add lettuce and tomatoes if you like. Enjoy!.
