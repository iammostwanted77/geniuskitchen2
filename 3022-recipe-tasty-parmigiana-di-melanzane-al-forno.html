Parmigiana di melanzane al forno step by step

  1. Cook tomato sauce, with a drizzle of oil, garlic some salt and some basil leaves. Cook for half an hour..
  2. Wash the two aubergines, cut into slices a centimeter and a half thick, Place on oven tray with parchment paper. Sprinkle some salt and olive oil. Put them in the oven at 190 ° on the grill for about ten, fifteen minutes. Eggplants should soften but not darken..
  3. Slice the mozzarella and grate the parmesan. In baking dish spread some olive oil and begin to alternate layers of aubergines, mozzarella, tomato sauce and grated parmesan Finish with a layer of Sauce and plenty of grated cheese..
  4. Bake at 200 degrees for 30 minutes until a crust has made on the surface. Leave to rest for at least half an hour before serving..
