Ingredients of Buttermilk Pie crust

  1. It's 2 C of all purpose flour.
  2. It's 1 t of salt.
  3. It's 2/3 C of vegetable shortening.
  4. It's 3 T of cold butter.
  5. You need 1/3 C of buttermilk, or as needed.
  6. You need 2 t of vegetable oil.

Buttermilk Pie crust step by step

  1. Whisk salt and flour together in a bowl..
  2. With a pastry cutter, cut shortening and butter into flour mixture until chunks are the size of small peas..
  3. Stir in buttermilk and vegetable oil with a fork until dough is moistened and beginning to gather together..
  4. Form the dough by hand into a compact shape.
  5. Divide dough in half and roll each half into a ball..
  6. Roll out each ball into required size and shape between two sheets of parchment paper..
