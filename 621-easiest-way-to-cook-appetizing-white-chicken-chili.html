Ingredients of White Chicken Chili

  1. You need 4 each of chicken breasts.
  2. Prepare 2 each of green bell peppers.
  3. It's 1 head of yellow onion.
  4. It's 1 each of jalapeño pepper.
  5. It's 1 1/2 tbsp of butter.
  6. You need 28 oz of white beans.
  7. It's 3 1/2 cup of chicken broth.
  8. Prepare 1 tsp of chili powder.
  9. Prepare 1/4 tsp of cayenne pepper.
  10. It's 1 tsp of salt.
  11. It's 1 1/4 cup of milk.
  12. You need 1/4 cup of cornmeal.

White Chicken Chili step by step

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil..
  2. Add chicken breasts and cook for 30 minutes until coined through..
  3. When cool, shred chicken breasts and set aside..
  4. Combine cornmeal and milk and set aside..
  5. Melt butter in a large pot over medium high heat and add diced peppers and onions. Saute until soft, about 8-10 minutes..
  6. Add beans, chicken, broth, and spices. Stir to combine and bring to a low boil. Cover and simmer for 20-30 minutes..
  7. Add cornmeal and milk mixture. Simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat and let stand for 10 minutes..
  8. Top with sour cream, cilantro, and tortilla chips (optional)..
