  7. You need 1/2 cup of coarsely broken up pecans.

Granny's Toasted Coconut-Pecan Pie step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. Thoroughly combine eggs, sugar, butter, lemon, and vanilla.
  3. Stir in coconut and pecans. Pour into the pie shell..
  4. Bake for 45-50 minutes or until filling is set. Cool pie..
  5. Garnish with sweetened whipped cream and pecan halves if desired..
