Shallot & Bacon Crema instructions

  1. Slowly render fat from bacon in a small saute pan. When bacon is crispy, remove with a slotted spoon and set aside..
  2. Add shallots to hot bacon fat and caramelize very slowly. Add a small pinch of kosher salt. Add extra bacon fat, butter, or oil to shallots if additional fat is necessary to avoid burning shallots..
  3. When shallots are 95% caramelized, add sugar and stir. Remove from heat after 30 seconds. Lay shallots flat on a small plate or bowl and place in refrigerator for 30 minutes to cool..
  4. When shallots are completely cooled, add to sour cream with crispy diced bacon. Stir with a small spoon..
