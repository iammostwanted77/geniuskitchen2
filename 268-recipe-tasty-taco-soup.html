Ingredients of Taco Soup

  1. It's 1 lb of ground beef.
  2. You need 1 can of whole kernel corn (pour off water).
  3. It's 1 can of rotel tomatoes diced.
  4. It's 1 can of stewed tomatoes diced.
  5. It's 1 can of ranch style beans.
  6. You need 1 can of pinto beans.
  7. You need 1 pkg of ranch dressing mix.
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp of salt.
  9. You need 1 tbsp of pepper.
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp of garlic salt.
  11. Prepare 1 tbsp of chile power.
  12. It's 1 of diced onion.
  13. You need 3-4 cloves of garlic diced.
