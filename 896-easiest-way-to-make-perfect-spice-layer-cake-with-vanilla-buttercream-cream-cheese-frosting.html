Ingredients of Spice Layer Cake with Vanilla Buttercream Cream Cheese Frosting

  1. It's 2 1/2 cup of all-purpose flour.
  2. It's 1/4 cup of cornstarch.
  3. It's 4 tsp of baking powder.
  4. It's 1/2 tsp of salt.
  5. Prepare 2 tsp of ground ginger.
  6. You need 1 tsp of ground cinnamon.
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp of ground nutmeg.
  8. It's 1/2 tsp of ground cloves.
  9. You need 1 cup of whole milk.
  10. It's 3 of large eggs.
  11. It's 1 tsp of vanilla extract.
  12. You need 1 cup of unsalted butter ( 2 sticks ), at room temperature.
  13. It's 2 cup of granulated sugar.
  14. Prepare 1/2 cup of raisins ( optional ).
  15. Prepare 1 of recipe Vanilla Cream Cheese Buttercream Frosting, recipe attached below.
  16. You need 1 tbsp of cinnamon sugar, recipe attached below.
  17. You need 1 tbsp of chopped pecans.

Spice Layer Cake with Vanilla Buttercream Cream Cheese Frosting step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 350. Spray 2 - 9 inch cake pans with non stick spray..
  2. In a medium bowl whisk flour, cornstarch, baking powder, salt, ginger, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves, mix well and set aside..
  3. In a large bowl beat butter and sugar until light and fluffy, about 4 to 5 minutes, add eggs, milk and vanilla beat until combined. Add flour mixture mixing just until combined, do not over mix., fold in raisins, if using..
  4. Divide batter evenly in prepared pans. Bake 25 to 30 minutes until a toothpick inseted in center comes out clean..
  5. Cool in pans on rack about 15 minutes, then remove from pans and cool completely..
  6. For a four layer cake, using a sharp large knife cut each ccooled cake in half, to make four layers.
  7. Place one layer of cake on serving plate, frost top with 1/4 of the Vanilla Buttercream Cream Cheese Frosting, add another layer and frost top, add third layer and frost top, finally add top layer and frost top of that.   8. Sprinkle with cinnamon sugar and chopped pecans. https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/350614-cinnamon-sugar.
  9. Because of the cream cheese in the frosting, this cake is best stored in refrigerator, but served at room temperature..
