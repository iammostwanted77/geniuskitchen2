Pie dough cinnamon rolls instructions

  1. preheat oven to 350.
  2. cut shortening or lard into flour, salt and 1tbs cinnamon.
  3. slowly add water till dough sticks into a ball be careful not to over water to where its super sticky. you also don't want a dry dough.
  4. roll dough into two large rectangles.
  5. melt then spread butter or margin on pieces of dough.
  6. sprinkle sugar and cinnamon as desired.
  7. if desired add nuts.
  8. roll dough onto its self like a roll.
  9. cut rolls into individual serving.
  10. place on greased cookie sheet.
  11. bake for 13 minutes or till golden brown.
