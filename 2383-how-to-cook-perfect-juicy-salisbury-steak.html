Juicy Salisbury Steak instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. Mix beef, onion soup, stuffing & water.
  3. Shape into oval shaped patties.
  4. Bake for 45 minutes or until juices run clear.
  5. Once cooked, drain fat, add gravy. Bake an additional 15 minutes.
