Ingredients of Hot chocolate french toast

  1. Prepare 4 slice of Bread.
  2. Prepare 1 cup of Milk.
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp of Corn flour.
  4. You need 2 tbsp of Cocoa powder.
  5. It's 3 tbsp of Sugar powder.
  6. Prepare 50 gm of Butter.
  7. It's 2 tbsp of Chocolate sauce.
