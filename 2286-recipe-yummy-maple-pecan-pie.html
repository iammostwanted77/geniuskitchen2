Ingredients of Maple-Pecan Pie

  1. It's 1 of 9" pie crust.
  2. You need 2 cup of pure maple syrup.
  3. You need 2 of eggs, lightly beaten.
  4. It's 1/4 cup of firmly packed light or dark brown sugar.
  5. Prepare 1/4 tsp of salt.
  6. You need 2 tbsp of unsalted butter melted.
  7. It's 1 tsp of vanilla extract.
  8. Prepare 1 1/2 cup of pecans, coarsely chopped.

Maple-Pecan Pie step by step

  1. prebake crust for 20 min, or until not wet at 375°F.
  2. Lower T to 350..
  3. In pan, bring maple syrup to a boil for 8-10 min until reduced to about 1 1/2 cups. Let cool..
  4. In bowl, combine eggs, brown sugar, maple syrup, salt, melted butter, vanilla until well combined..
  5. Add pecans and stir well..
  6. Pour filling into pie shell, spread pecans evenly..
  7. Bake pie until center is slightly puffed and firm to touch ( about 30-35 min)..
  8. Let cool and serve slightly warm..
