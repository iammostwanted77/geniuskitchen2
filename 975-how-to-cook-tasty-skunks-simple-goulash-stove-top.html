Ingredients of skunks simple goulash stove top

  1. You need 1 1/2 lb of ground beef.
  2. It's 1 box of macaroni 16 ounces.
  3. You need 1 tsp of turmeric.
  4. It's 1/4 cup of sugar.
  5. It's 2 can of tomato sauce 12 ounces each.
  6. It's 2 can of tomato paste 8 ounces each.
  7. It's 2 tsp of salt or to taste.
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp of white pepper.
  9. It's 2 tbsp of dried parsley flakes.
  10. It's 1 tsp of sweet dried basil.
  11. It's 2 cup of pasta water boiling.
  12. You need 1/2 cup of grated Romano Cheese.
  13. You need 8 oz of for topping white extra sharp shredded cheddar cheese about 1/4 cup each.
  14. You need 1 large of onion chopped.

skunks simple goulash stove top instructions

  1. in a big pot add about 6 quarts water 2 tablespoonfuls of olive oil 2 tablespoonfuls of salt bring to boil add pasta bring back to boil boil 7-9 minutes till done.
  2. while pasta water is heating in a pan add beef onion spices after brown add sauce and paste add water stir well.
  3. drain pasta add sauce add Romano cheese stir well turn off heat serve with shredded white cheddar cheese.
