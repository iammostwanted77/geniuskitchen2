Ingredients of philly cheese steak stuffed peppers

  1. You need 8 oz of thinly sliced roast beef.
  2. It's 8 slice of provolone cheese.
  3. Prepare 2 large of Bell peppers.
  4. It's 1 medium of Sweet onion sliced.
  5. It's 6 oz of Baby Bella mushrooms sliced.
  6. It's 2 tbsp of butter.
  7. It's 2 tbsp of olive oil.
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp of garlic minced.

philly cheese steak stuffed peppers instructions

  1. preheat oven to 400.
  2. slice peppers in half length wise and remove ribs and seeds.
  3. in large saute pan over low medium heat add butter, olive oil, garlic, mushrooms, onions, and salt and pepper to taste. saute until onions and mushrooms are Caramelized. bout 30 min..
  4. add sliced roast beef to mushroom/onion mix and allow to cook 5-10 min.
  5. line ea pepper with a slice of cheese. stuff with meat mixture until nearly overflowing and top with another slice of cheese.
  6. bake for 15-20 min until cheese on top is golden brown.
