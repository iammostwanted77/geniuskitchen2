Indian Kheer step by step

  1. Put rice cardamom seeds and milk in a sauce pot and cook on the lowest heat possible till rice is tender..
  2. Add in raisins and take out cardamom seeds.
  3. Add in sugar.
  4. Top with more milk and raisins.
  5. Serve.
