Hummingbird Poke Cake step by step

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C and grease a 9x13 casserole dish..
  2. Combine flour, white and brown sugars, baking soda, walnuts, and cinnamon in a large mixing bowl..
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together oil, eggs, vanilla essance, crushed pineapple and mashed bananas. Whisk until all combined..
  4. Pour wet ingredients into the dry ingredients. Mix until smooth..
  5. Pour cake batter into the prepared dish and spread it evenly. Bake for 40 minutes..
  6. While the cake is baking, make the sweet sauce. In a mixing bowl, whisk together condensed milk, pineapple, cinnamon and Orley Whip..
  7. Let the cake cool for about 10 minutes. Poke holes (pencil thickness) all over the cake..
  8. Evenly, pour sweet sauce all over the cake. Let it seep in and cool completely before frosting..
  9. To make the frosting, beat together the butter and cream cheese and then the icing sugar, cinnamon and vanilla essence until the frosting is light, smooth and fluffy..
  10. Once the cake is cooled, evenly frost the cake all over and add the chopped walnuts all over the top of the frosted cake..
