Cinnamon Pecan Pie instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F..
  2. In a large mixing bowl, mix together filling ingredients thoroughly..
  3. Spread all but a handful of the pecan halves into pie shell..
  4. Pour filling mixture over the pecans in the pie shell. Top with remaining handful of pecan halves..
  5. Bake at 350°F 30-45 minutes or until toothpick comes clean out of center..
