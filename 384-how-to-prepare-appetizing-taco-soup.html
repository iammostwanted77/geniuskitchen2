Taco soup step by step

  1. cook meat, onions & garlic till done.
  2. drain grease add taco mix with a little water to mix.
  3. to save time while your meat is cooking open cans of beans and juice & corn & put in a big stew pot & put on simmer.
  4. once meat is cooked pour into pot with all the other stuff.
  5. let cook for atleast 30 minutes.
  6. when ready take Frito's place in bowl pour soup over top sprinkle cheese & top with sour cream (salsa optional).
  7. eat & enjoy, good for groups of ppl our when you want food for the week, great when its cold out, & can also spice up as needed for you spicey fans :).
