Chicken Soup step by step

  1. With the help of a sharp knife cut the chicken into smaller pieces..
  2. Heat the oil in the pot over medium-high heat until shimmering. Add the carcass pieces and cook, turning occasionally, until lightly browned all over, about 8 minutes..
  3. Add the remaining ingredients and stir to combine, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the pot with a wooden spoon. Bring to a simmer (do not let the stock come to a boil)..
  4. Reduce the heat to low and allow to simmer, until the flavors have developed, about 40-45 minutes..
  5. Set a fine-mesh strainer and pour the stock through the strainer Discard the contents of the strainer..
  6. Bring the stock to a simmer over medium-high heat. Add the carrots, onion, saltand season with pepper, and stir to combine. Return to a simmer, reduce the heat to medium low, and simmer until the vegetables are tender, about 20 minutes..
  7. Meanwhile, bring a medium saucepan of heavily salted water to a boil over high heat. Add the noodles and cook it. Drain in a colander..
  8. When the vegetables are tender, add the drained noodles and shredded chicken, stir to combine, and allow to simmer. Cook until the flavors meld, about 5 minutes more. Adjust the Taste and season with salt and pepper..
