Oreo cake instructions

  1. Add allbthe cookies in a mixer jar and coarsely grind it.
  2. Pour this mixture in a bowl and add pwdered sugar,baking powder and milk to make a nice batter..
  3. Grease the mould with butter or oil and sprinkle some refined flour. preheat the wok and place the stand in centre of wok. Now place the mould on that stand and let it bake on medium low flame for 25-30 min. By covering a lid..
