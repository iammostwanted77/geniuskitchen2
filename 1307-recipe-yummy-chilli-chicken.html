Recipe: Yummy Chilli Chicken

Chilli Chicken.

You can have Chilli Chicken using 16 ingredients and 19 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Chilli Chicken

  1. You need 500 grams of Chicken (curry pieces).
  2. Prepare 1 of Onion , finely chopped.
  3. Prepare 3 of Onion , cut in rings.
  4. You need 1 of Capsicum , large Â  cubes.
  5. Prepare 1 of Green Chilli , finely chopped.
  6. It's 2 - 3 of Green Chilli , whole.
  7. It's 2 tbsps of Garlic Paste.
  8. It's 10 cloves of Garlic , finely chopped - 12.
  9. You need 1/2 tsp of Ginger , finely chopped.
  10. Prepare 1.5 tsps of Green Chilli Sauce (optional).
  11. It's 1 tbsp of Vinegar.
  12. It's 4 tbsps of Soya Sauce.
  13. You need 1 tbsps of cornflour.
  14. It's 1/4 cup of White Oil.
  15. You need 1/3 cup of Water.
  16. You need to taste of Salt.

Chilli Chicken step by step

  1. 2-3 hours before you plan to start cooking, marinate the chicken with 2 tbsp of soya sauce and 1 tsp of salt. Let the chicken rest at room temperature..
  2. After 2-3 hours, heat a few tbsp of oil in a wok. Add in the capsicum pieces, and lightly saute. Remove, and keep aside..
  3. To the remaining oil, add the finely chopped onion, ginger, garlic, and green chilli. Saute on medium heat..
  4. Now add the ginger and garlic pastes. Stir for 2-3 mins..
  5. Finely add in the marinated chicken pieces to the wok. Mix well..
  6. Pour in the green chilli sauce, if using..
  7. Fry the chicken pieces till they slightly brown. This should take about 7-8 mins..
  8. Finally pour in the water. Add salt. Let the gravy simmer for 8-10mins..
  9. Once the chicken is almost done, add the vinegar and 1 tbsp soya sauce..
  10. In a separate bowl, mix the cornflour in 3-4 tbsp of water..
  11. Pour this into the gravy..
  12. Add in the capsicum..
  13. Now cook everything for 1-2 mins. The gravy will thicken..
  14. Your dish is now 90% done!.
  15. Just before serving, in a frying pan, add oil and fry the onion rings and the whole green chillies..
  16. Add 1 tbsp of soya sauce and a bit of salt to enhance the colour and taste..
  17. Now add all this to the wok..
  18. Turn the stove on. Mix the gravy till everything becomes well incorporated..
  19. Your delicious chilli chicken is now ready! Enjoy!!.
