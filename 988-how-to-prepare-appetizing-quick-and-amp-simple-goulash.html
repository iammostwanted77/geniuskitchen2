Ingredients of Quick & Simple Goulash

  1. You need 1.5 lb of ground beef.
  2. You need 2 cans of diced tomatoes, approx 30oz.
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp of chili powder.
  4. It's 5 cloves of garlic, or approx 2 tsp granulated garlic.
  5. You need 1 tsp of paprika.
  6. It's 1 tsp of black pepper.
  7. You need Pinch of sugar.
  8. It's to taste of Salt.
  9. It's .5 tsp of cayenne pepper.
  10. You need 1 cup of water.
  11. Prepare 2 cups of macaroni noodle.

Quick & Simple Goulash step by step

  1. Brown meat over high heat with a pinch of salt until most of the water has evaporated..
  2. If using fresh garlic, add for a minute to sweat a little. Otherwise add the granulated garlic along with the chili powder, paprika, black and cayenne pepper and let cook for just a minute to wake up the spice aromas..
  3. Add diced tomatoes, sugar, and cup of water, then bring to a low boil on medium high heat. There should be slightly to much liquid so you can reduce it for the next 15 minutes. If it gets too dry, add a little water..
  4. Boil water for noodles. I like to add salt and olive oil to the water as it comes to a boil. Once boil is reached, add noodles..
  5. Boil until just before noodles are done. The liquid in the porridge will finish them. Otherwise they tend to be overcooked. Drain the noodles and combine with the porridge..
  6. Let stand a few minutes, then serve..
