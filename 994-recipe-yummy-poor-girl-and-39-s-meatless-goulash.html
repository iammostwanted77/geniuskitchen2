Ingredients of Poor Girl's Meatless Goulash

  1. Prepare 1 can of kidney beans (.75 cents).
  2. Prepare 1 can of diced tomatoes and green chilies (.55 cents).
  3. Prepare 1 can of corn (.50. cents).
  4. It's 1 cup of elbow macaroni ($1.00).
  5. You need 1 can of spaghetti sauce ($1.00).
  6. Prepare to taste of Italian seasoning.
  7. You need to taste of salt and pepper.
  8. You need 1 tsp of garlic powder.

Poor Girl's Meatless Goulash step by step

  1. Combine corn, diced tomatoes, spaghetti sauce, beans, seasoning, and cooked pasta in a large pot..
  2. Bring to a slow boil, reduce heat. Serve with garlic bread and enjoy!.
