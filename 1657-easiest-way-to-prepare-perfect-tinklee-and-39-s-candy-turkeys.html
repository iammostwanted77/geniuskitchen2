Tinklee's Candy Turkeys instructions

  1. take 12 cookies and remove one side of the chocolate cookie and set it aside.
  2. now place a whole cookie at a slight tilt about halfway back on cookie.
  3. add your peanut butter cup by laying it flat against the oreo, so the bottom sticks out.
  4. with a lil bit of icing (I would do white and divide into 2 colors make brown, and orange) attach the malt ball/ turkey head, put a lil brown icing on the reese cup and on the cookie where the malt ball will touch and set it in place.
  5. take and stick 5 candy corn, white tip into the the filling of the cookie standing up, to mske the feathers, put one up top in the middle, then 2 more to each side.
  6. using brown icing take the white tip of a candy corn and glue it to the front of the malt ball, then with the orange icing make a lil ruffle under the malt ball on top of the reese cup.
