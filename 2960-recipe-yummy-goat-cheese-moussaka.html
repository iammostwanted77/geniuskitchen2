Ingredients of Goat cheese Moussaka

  4. It's 100 grams of lowfat fresh cheese.
  5. Prepare 1 of grated Parmesan.
  6. It's 1 of olive oil.
  7. Prepare 1 of fresh basil.
  8. You need 1 of cumin.
  9. You need 1 of salt, pepper.

Goat cheese Moussaka step by step

  1. preheat oven to 302°F.
  2. cut the eggplant into thin slice in the lengthwise.
  3. in a nonstick skillet, fry the eggplant slices on each side a few min. in a little oil, some salt and pepper and set aside on a plate covered with paper towels.
  4. in a bowl, mix the goat cheese with cumin seeds and chopped fresh basil, lightly salt and pepper.
  5. in a baking dish, alternate a layer of eggplant, tomato coulis and goat cheese.
  6. finish with a layer of eggplant and a layer of the Parmesan and fresh cheese mixed.
  7. bake for 20 min.
  8. you can serve this dish with a lightly seasoned salad.
