Ingredients of white chicken chili

  1. Prepare 3 each of frozen chicken breasts.
  2. You need 1 can of kernel corn, not drained.
  3. You need 1 can of rotel tomatoes and chilies, undrained.
  4. It's 1 can of black beans, drained & rinsed.
  5. You need 1 envelope of hidden valley ranch dry mix.
  6. Prepare 8 oz of cream cheese.
  7. It's 1 tbsp of chili powder.
  8. It's 1 tsp of cumin.

white chicken chili instructions

  1. Put frozen chicken in the bottom of crock pot..
  2. Pour corn, tomatoes, beans, ranch mix and spices on top..
  3. Place cream cheese block on top...
  4. Cook on low for 6-7 hours. Mix all ingredients. Pull apart chicken..
  5. Serve with nacho chips..
