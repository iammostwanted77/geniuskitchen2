Philly Cheese Steak Stuffed-crust Pizza instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F..
  2. Spray pizza pan with cooking spray and lightly dust with cornmeal..
  3. Roll out pizza crust. Cut string cheese in half and roll around edges of crust and pinch to close..
  4. Spread softened boursin over crust and then spread crema. Layer provolone, then spread desired amount of philly steak, chopped mushrooms and sliced onions. Sprinkle monterrey jack over top..
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until crust golden brown and fillings hot and bubbly..
