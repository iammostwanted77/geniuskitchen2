Aubergine mini pizzas step by step

  1. PREPARE THE EGGPLANT (look up on YouTube).
  2. Cut eggplant in slices of 1 cm and let them dry on a paper towel for 10 min.
  3. Coat in olive oil (mix olive oil with crushed garlic for more flavour) and put in oven until they start to brown/gold.
  4. Make the tomato sauce: garlic first then everything else.
  5. Put the sauce on top of the aubergines & cover with some mozzarella and Parmesan (make sure the sauce isn’t too dry because it will dry in the oven too).
  6. Put back in the oven for 15 min.
  7. Serve with fresh basil leaves.
