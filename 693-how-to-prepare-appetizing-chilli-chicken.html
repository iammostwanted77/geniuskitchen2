Ingredients of Chilli chicken

  1. Prepare 1 cup of boneless chicken.
  2. It's 1 of egg white.
  3. You need 4 tsp of red chilli sauce.
  4. You need 3 tsp of cornflour.
  5. It's 1 tbsp of soya sauce.
  6. Prepare 1 tsp of white vinegar1 tbsp tomato ketchup.
  7. Prepare 3 tbsp of oil.
  8. It's to taste of salt.
  9. You need 1 of onion cubed.
  10. It's 1/2 cup of green capsicum cubed.
  11. Prepare 1 tsp of ginger garlic paste.
  12. It's 1/2 cup of water.
  13. You need of oil for deep frying.

Chilli chicken instructions

  1. Wash & rinse the chicken & cut them in to small pieces.
  2. Take a bowl add chicken,salt, egg white & Cornflour,Marinate for an hour.
  3. Take another bowl add red chilli sauce,white vinegar,soya sauce & tomato ketchup.Mix them & set aside.
  4. Take a non stick pan,add oil & deep fry the marinated chicken pieces on low flame till they become light pink in colour.
  5. Take a karahi add little oil & put ginger garlic paste & onions..
  6. Cook till the onions becomes translucent & add capsicum on a high flame..
  7. Now add sauce mixture & chicken pieces & mix well..
  8. Add water & cook till it is absorbed totally..
  9. Garnish with coriander leaves & serve hot.
