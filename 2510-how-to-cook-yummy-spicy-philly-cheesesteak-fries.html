Ingredients of Spicy Philly Cheesesteak Fries

  1. Prepare 1 packages of frozen fries.
  2. You need 1 packages of steak ums steak.
  3. You need 1 small of diced or sliced onion.
  4. It's 1 small of diced or sliced green or red pepper (I like red bc they become a little sweet).
  5. Prepare 1 dash of season salt.
  6. It's 1 dash of ground black pepper.
  7. It's 1/2 packages of shredded pepper jack cheese.
  8. It's 1/2 stick of butter.
  9. Prepare 1 of oil for frying.

Spicy Philly Cheesesteak Fries instructions

  1. Fry fries until crispy & place on paper towel on a plate to soak up excess oil..
  2. Meanwhile, heat butter in nonstick skillet & add onion, pepper, & seasonings..
  3. When veggies almost translucent, add steak-ums. Cook until done..
  4. Preheeat oven 375°. Place fries on a baking sheet. Top with steak mixture first then cover with cheese..
  5. Bake for 20 minutes or until cheese melts. Then serve as a snack or side dish..
