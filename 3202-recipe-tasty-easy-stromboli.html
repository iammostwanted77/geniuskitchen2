Ingredients of Easy Stromboli

  1. You need 1 of Pillsbury Pizza dough.
  2. It's 1 of pack of sliced provolone.
  3. It's 1 of pack of peperroni.
  4. You need 1 of pack of shredded mozzerella.

  6. You need 1 of olive oil, extra virgin.
  7. You need 1 of oregano.

Easy Stromboli step by step

  1. Rub thin layer of oil onto pan, roll out pizza dough..
  2. Put slices of provolone down middle of the dough, over lapping each other..
  3. Put your ham on, only 10 slices are needed..
  4. Put your peperroni on, covering the ham..
  5. Sprinkle 3 handfuls of mozzerella over the pepperoni..
  6. Fold your dough into a overlapping form. Covering all ingredients..
  7. Now brush olive oil over the top and sprinkle oregano over it.
  8. Cook for 8 minutes or until it is at your desired feel..
  9. Last, Serve and enjoy!.
