  4. Prepare 1 can of diced tomatos.

  6. You need 12 oz of V8.
  7. It's 1 of hot sauce to taste.

goulash step by step

  1. boil noodles according to directions on box.
  2. in a large skillet or pan cook onion, meat, hamburger seasoning. when meat is cooked all the way through drain the fat.
  3. add diced tomatoes (undrained) to the meat.
  4. drain noodles and add to meat mixture.
  5. add V8 to the mixture, you can use more or less than 12 oz depending how thick you want it.
  6. add hot sauce to taste and cover and let it simmer on medium to medium low for 10 minutes stirring every so often.
  7. enjoy.
