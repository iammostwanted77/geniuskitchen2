Chocolate Covered Oreo Cream Cheese Balls instructions

  1. Put the Oreo cookies in a large zip lock back and crush them with a rolling pin. You can also use store packaged Oreo cookie crumbs..
  2. Put the cookie crumbs in a large bowl and mash them so they are small. Add the cream cheese to it and mix well..
  3. Roll the mixture into balls. Freeze for 15 minutes..
  4. Melt the chocolate in a double boiler or in the microwave (2 mins). Be careful and do not burn the chocolate. Once the chocolate is all melted dip each ball into the chocolate and place in a baking sheet covered with parchment/wax paper..
  5. Put in the fridge so that the chocolate will set..
  6. Done!!!!!!.
