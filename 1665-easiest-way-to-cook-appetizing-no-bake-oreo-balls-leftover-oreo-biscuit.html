  2. Prepare 1/2 cup of milk.
  3. Prepare 2 of Dairy milk Chocolate (40 RS each).
  4. It's as required of Milkmaid (to drizzle).
  5. It's as required of Chocalate sprinkles.

No Bake Oreo Balls - leftover Oreo Biscuit instructions

  1. Take the dairy milk chocolate and melt it with the help of a double boiler. Once done keep it aside.
  2. Now take the leftover oreo biscuit and grind it into the mixer..
  3. Take a mixing bowl add ground Oreo,2 tbsp dairy milk chocolate and add milk as required to knead the dough..
  4. Once the dough is kneaded make small balls out of it and dip them into the chocolate sauce and place it on the serving plate..
  5. Once all the balls are done drizzle milkmaid and sprinkle the chocolate sprinkler on it and refrigerate it for 20 to 30 mins until the chocolate is completely set..
  6. Your Oreo balls are ready to eat..
