Homemade cranberry sauce instructions

  1. Bring the orange juice to a boil..
  2. Add cranberries and sugar.
  3. Reduce heat to low..
  4. Cook for 1 hour or more until reduced to 1/3 volume. Stirring occasionally to prevent sticking..
  5. Remove from heat and chill until ready to serve..
  6. Enjoy!.
