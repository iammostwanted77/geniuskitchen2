How to Prepare Yummy Apple and pecan stuffed /baked sweet potatoes

Delicious, fresh and tasty.

Apple and pecan stuffed /baked sweet potatoes.

You can have Apple and pecan stuffed /baked sweet potatoes using 8 ingredients and 5 steps. Here is how you cook it.

Ingredients of Apple and pecan stuffed /baked sweet potatoes

  1. It's 6 each of med sweet potatoes.
  2. It's 1/2 cup of cubed butter.
  3. It's 1/4 cup of packed brown sugar.
  4. It's 1/2 tsp of grated orange peel.
  5. It's 1 each of medium apple chopped.
  6. You need 1/4 cup of chopped pecans.
  7. It's 1 cup of shredded cheese of choice for topping if desired.
  8. It's 1 cup of mini marshmallos if desired.

Apple and pecan stuffed /baked sweet potatoes step by step

  1. prep time 45 mins, bake approx 1 and 1/2 hours total plus cooling.
  2. scrub and pierce potatoes with a fork. place in foil lined 15 X10 by 1inch baking pan. bake at 400degrees for 45~60 minutes or until tender.
  3. when cool, cut a thin slice from top of each potato; discard. scoop out pulp, leaving 1/4 inch thick shells; transfer to a large bowl. mash with butter, brown sugar and orange peel. fold in apple and pecans.( if desired and you wish, you could add a little more brown sugar or butter to taste: I did, just a bit).
  4. spoon into potatoes shells. return to baking pan. bake 15~20 mins longer or until heated through..
  5. if you choose to top with shredded cheese , or marshmallows, you could add it during the last step to melt :-).
