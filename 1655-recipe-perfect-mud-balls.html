Ingredients of Mud Balls

  1. Prepare 1 packages of of Oreos.
  2. You need 8 oz of Cream Cheese.
  3. Prepare 1 packages of chocolate chips.

Mud Balls instructions

  1. Crush Oreos up into a fine grind using food processor/blender.
  2. Combine with cream cheese.
  3. Form into balls.
  4. Stick toothpicks into balls.
  5. Put into refrigerator to harden up.
  6. After balls are hardened, melt chocolate chips in microwave (Or Oven).
  7. COMPLETELY cover ball with melted chocolate.
  8. Put into refrigerator to hardened.
  9. Twist: vanilla Oreos with White Chocolate chips, Use Peanut Butter in the ball Forming stages, Use mint chocolate chips, use dark chocolate chips.
