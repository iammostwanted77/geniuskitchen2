Ingredients of Eggplant Parmesan Pasta

  1. It's 1 of egg.
  2. You need 1/2 of inch eggplant slices.
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp of panko.
  4. Prepare 1 of salt and pepper to taste.
  5. It's 1 of baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. You need 2 oz of whole wheat angel hair pasta.
  7. It's 1/2 cup of marinara sauce.
  8. It's 2 tbsp of torn basil leaves.
  9. It's 1 of grated parmesan.

Eggplant Parmesan Pasta step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Beat 1 egg and dip eggplant in egg, coat with panko and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Place on baking sheet and bake 10 minutes..
  4. Cook pasta and toss with marinara. Top with eggplant, basil and parmesan..
