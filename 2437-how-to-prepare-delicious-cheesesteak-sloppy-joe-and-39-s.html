Cheesesteak sloppy Joe's instructions

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, heat vegetable oil. Add onion and bell peppers and cook, stirring often, until softened, about 5 minutes..
  2. Stir in the garlic and cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds, then add the beef. Cook until no longer pink, 6 to 8 minutes. Drain fat.
  3. Stir in beef broth, ketchup, brown sugar, and Worcestershire and season generously with salt and pepper. Simmer until most the liquid has reduced and the mixture is thick, 5 minutes..
  4. Top beef with provolone and cover skillet with a tight-fitting lid to melt, about 2 minutes..
  5. Remove from heat and serve cheesy ground beef mixture in hamburger buns. Serve warm..
