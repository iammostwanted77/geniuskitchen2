Ingredients of Chocolate pecan pie

  1. It's 1 cup of Corn syrup.
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp of Softened butter.
  3. Prepare 2 tsp of Cornstarch.
  4. It's 1 tsp of Vanilla.
  5. It's 1 1/2 cup of Pecans (chopped).
  6. Prepare 1 of Unbaked deep dish pie crust.
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp of Salt.
  8. It's 1/2 cup of Sugar.
  9. Prepare 3 of Eggs (lightly beaten).
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp of White vinegar.
  11. It's 1/2 cup of Dark chocolate chips.

Chocolate pecan pie instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. Cream butter and sugar, then add beaten eggs, cornstarch, salt, vanilla, vinegar and corn syrup.
  3. Cut pastry dough 1 inch larger than pan to fold edges under and flute. Glaze bottom with egg white. Sprinkle pecans and chocolate on pie shell.
  4. Pour pie filing into crust.
  5. Bake on center each for 40 to 50 mins our until set. Cool on wire rack..
