home fried green tomatoes step by step

  1. preheat pan with vegetable oil for medium- high heat. it should be ready to fry when it pops and sizzles..
  2. mix the milk, egg and pepper in a bowl..
  3. in a blender or chopper, just blend all the breading ingredients evenly and finely.
  4. chop your green tomato in thick slices, should give you 6 good sized slices..
  5. dip tomatoe slices one by one in base, then evenly coat with beading.
  6. lightly place tomatoes on hot oiled pan and fry on each side for a few minutes on each side, until it has a light golden crispy skin.
  7. they will be oily unless you place a paper towel on a plate to let the tomatoes rest on when they are done cooking.
