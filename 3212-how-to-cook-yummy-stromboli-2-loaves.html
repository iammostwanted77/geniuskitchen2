Stromboli (2 Loaves) step by step

  1. prep pizza dough (stretch out oval shape-wax paper-flour).
  2. lay out ham first (fold slices 6).
  3. lay out white cheese on top of ham (~8 slices over lapping).
  4. lay salami on top (6 slices).
  5. lay mozzarella on top (4 slices).
  6. pull lightly sides of dough, fold over the top. pinch and twist to hold together.
  7. Put on tin foil, spray with Pam. put onto cookie sheet.
  8. preheat oven 350.
  9. after 5 mins brush butter on top and sides (not bottom to prevent burning).
  10. cook for ~20-25 mins until brown lightly and cheese melting on top.
  11. heat marinara sauce to serve on side.
