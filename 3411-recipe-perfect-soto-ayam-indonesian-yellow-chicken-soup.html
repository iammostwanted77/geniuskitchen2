Ingredients of Soto Ayam (Indonesian Yellow Chicken Soup)

  1. Prepare 500 ml of vegetable oil to fry the chicken.
  2. You need 1 of whole chicken, divided into 4.
  3. You need 2 of lt water.
  4. Prepare 8 of kaffir lime leaves.
  5. It's 4 stalks of lemongrass, take the white part only (which is on the bottom), pounded.
  6. You need 4 of green onions, chopped.
  7. Prepare 1,5 tsp of salt or to taste.
  8. You need 1/2 tsp of white pepper powder or to taste.
  9. You need 1/2 tsp of chicken bouillon.
  10. You need 1 Tbsp of vegetable oil.
  11. You need of Ground spices: (blend in a food processor).
  12. It's 7 cloves of garlic.
  13. It's 8 cloves of shallots.
  14. You need 3 pcs of candle nuts.
  15. It's 1 tsp of nutmegs powder.
  16. You need 3 cm of turmeric.
  17. Prepare 2 cm of ginger.
  18. It's of Chili sauce: Boil 5 bird eye chili peppers + 2 red chili peppers + 5 cloves garlic until soft. Remove from the heat. Grind all the ingredients using a mortar and pestle. Add 1/4 tsp salt.
  19. Prepare of Koya topping: Grind 5 cloves fried garlic + 8 fried prawn crackers using a mortal and pestle.
  20. Prepare of For the complement:.
  21. Prepare 4 of hard boiled eggs, peeled, cut.
  22. It's 50 gr of mung bean vermicelli, soaked in boiled water for 3 minutes, removed from the heat, drained.
  23. Prepare of some lime wedges.
