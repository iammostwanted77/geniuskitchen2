Ingredients of slow cooker family favorite pot roast

  1. You need 2 tsp of Olive or vegetable oil.
  2. Prepare 6 medium of potatoes, cut into 2 inch pieces(about 4 cups).
  3. It's 2 1/2 cup of Baby cut carrots.
  4. You need 2 cup of Sliced fresh mushrooms (about 5 ounces).
  5. It's 1 1/2 cup of medium stalk celery, sliced.
  6. It's 1 1/2 cup of medium onion chopped.
  7. Prepare 1 tsp of Salt.
  8. You need 1/2 tsp of pepper.
  9. It's 1/2 tsp of Dried thyme leaves.
  10. Prepare 1 can of Diced tomatoes, ingrained duo not drain.
  11. It's 1 can of Condensed beef consomme out broth.
  12. You need 1 can of V8 juice (2/3 cup).
  13. You need 1/4 cup of Gold medal flour.

slow cooker family favorite pot roast step by step

  1. Heat oil in 10 inch skillet over medium high heat. I beef roast comes in netting or is tied, do not remove. cook beef in oil about 10 minutes, turning occasionally, until brown on all sides..
  2. Place potatoes, carrots, mushrooms, celery and onions in 4 to 5 quart slow cooker. Sprinkle with salt, pepper and thyme. Place beef vegetables. Pour tomatoes, consomme, and vegetable juice over beef..
  3. Cover and cook on low seeing 8 to 10 hours..
  4. remove beef and vegetables from cooker; using slotted spoon; place on serving plaster platter and cover to warm. Skim fat from beef juices in cooker if desired. Remove 1/2 cup of the juices from cooker, mix with flour in small bowl, using wire whisk, until smooth. gradually stir flour mixture into remaining juices in cooker. Increase heat setting to high. Cover and cook 15 minutes or until tuckedthickened. Remove netting or strings from beef. Serve sauce with beef and vegetables..
