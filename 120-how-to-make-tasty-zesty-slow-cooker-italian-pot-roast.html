Ingredients of zesty slow cooker italian pot roast

  1. You need 2 1/2 of boneless beef pot roast.
  2. Prepare 1/2 tsp of black pepper.
  3. Prepare 4 medium of potatoes, cut in quarters.
  4. It's 2 cup of baby carrots.
  5. It's 1 of stock celery, cut into 1 in pieces.
  6. It's 1 medium of diced tomatoes.
  7. Prepare 10 3/4 can of tomato soup.
  8. You need 1 cup of water.
  9. You need 1 tbsp of dried basil leave.
  10. It's 1 tsp of dried oregano leaves.
  11. Prepare 1 tsp of dried parsley flakes, crushed.
  12. Prepare 1 tsp of vinegar.
