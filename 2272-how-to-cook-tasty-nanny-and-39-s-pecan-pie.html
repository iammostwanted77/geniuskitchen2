Ingredients of Nanny's Pecan Pie

  1. You need 3 of eggs.
  2. It's 1 cup of sugar.
  3. Prepare 1 pinch of salt.
  4. It's 1 cup of light Karo syrup.
  5. You need 3 tbsp of butter.
  6. It's 1 tsp of vanilla.
  7. You need 1 of deep pie crust.

Nanny's Pecan Pie step by step

  1. preheat oven to 350.
  2. beat eggs on high until pale yellow and frothy.
  3. keep beating while adding ingredients..
  4. add salt.
  5. add sugar.
  6. add Karo.
  7. add butter.
  8. add vanilla.
  9. place pecans (whole or chopped) in bottom of pie crust.
  10. pie filling mixture over pecans SLOWLY. pecans will rise.
  11. cook 45 minutes or until filling is set and no longer runny..
  12. if top is browning and needs to cook longer, decrease oven temp to 300 and cook another ten minutes or so..
