Ingredients of Crock pot roast

  1. It's 4 lb of chuck roast.
  2. Prepare 1 packages of dry onion soup mix.
  3. Prepare 1 cup of water.
  4. Prepare 3 of carrots chopped.
  5. It's 1 of onion chopped.
  6. You need 3 of potatoes peeled and cubed.
  7. You need 1 of stock celery chopped.
  8. Prepare 1 of salt and pepper to taste.

Crock pot roast step by step

  1. Season the roast with salt and pepper.
  2. Brown all sides in large skillet 4 min per side.
  3. Place roast in slow cooker, add soup mix, water, carrots, onion, potatoes, and celery.
  4. Cover and cook on low setting 8 to 10 hrs.
