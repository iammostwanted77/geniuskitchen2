Salisbury steak (Filipino style) instructions

  1. Make beef patties by mixing ground beef, salt, pepper, egg and bread crumbs..
  2. Fry the beef patties. And let it cool.
  3. Make the mushroom sauce by, heating the pan with butter, putting beef cube or beef broth and mushroom, stir, add the flour and corn flour continue mixing it while pouring milk. Then sprinkle salt n pepper for tasting..
