Ingredients of Arroz con leche

  1. It's 2 cup of whole milk.
  2. Prepare 1 1/4 cup of Jasmine white rice.
  3. Prepare 3 stick of cinnamon.
  4. Prepare 1 cup of sugar.
  5. It's 1 1/3 cup of water.

Arroz con leche instructions

  1. You will need milk water rice sugar cinnamon and Jasmine white rice.
  2. Mix all ingredients together in a med pot.
  3. Cook on med heat.
  4. Stir occasionally to prevent rice from getting stuck.
  5. Enjoy hot or cold.
