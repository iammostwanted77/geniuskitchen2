Leftover Chicken CheeseSteak step by step

  1. Wrap bread in foil place in oven at 400Â°F. No need to preheat. Keep in oven till everything is finished. For best quality.
  2. Toss bacon into medium sized pan add minced garlic. Mix em together.
  3. Shred your left over chicken. You can you ur hand. Once your bacon starts to show color add you chicken to a lower heat. Mix everything together.
  4. Add roast beef. Shredded as well mix everything together..
  5. Add unmelted butter to skillet let every simmer at a low heat.
  6. In a small bowl add melted butter, mayo, and mustard. Mix with a whisk really well. Add powdered garlic.
  7. Remove bread from oven. Place on serving plate.
  8. Had little sauce to bottom bread then mozzarella cheese.
  9. Add meat as filling.
  10. Had more sauce on top. Let it drizzle through.
  11. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese on top..
  12. Add one more pat of sauce on top bun and put it on the sandwich.
  13. Place remaining sauce in fridge for later.
  14. Enjoy your meal. This will be a messy delicious sandwich.
