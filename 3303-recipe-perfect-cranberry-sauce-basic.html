  2. Prepare 3/4 cup of sugar.
  3. Prepare 12 oz of cranberries.

cranberry sauce-basic instructions

  1. bring sugar and water to boil, stirring to dissolve sugar.
  2. add cranberries and bring back to boil.
  3. reduce heat and simmer for 10 mins or until berries start to burst.
  4. cool completely, then chill.
