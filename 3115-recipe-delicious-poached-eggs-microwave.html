Poached eggs - Microwave instructions

  1. Grease the microwave safe bowl with Oil. Break the egg in the bowl. sprinkle salt and dry herbs to taste,.
  2. Microwave for 20 sec at full power. wait for 10 sec. again microwave for 20 secs and wait for 10 sec. microwave again for 10 sec and let it set for 10 sec..
  3. Take out the bowl and serve to eat!!.
