Ingredients of Creamy shallot & mushroom fettuccine

  1. It's 400 grams of Fettuccine.
  2. It's 1 tbsp of Olive oil.
  3. Prepare 20 grams of Butter.
  4. Prepare 375 grams of Mushrooms, sliced.
  5. It's 8 of Shallots, peeled, cut in wedges.
  6. Prepare 2 clove of Garlic, sliced.
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp of Wholegrain mustard.
  8. You need 300 ml of Thickened cream.
