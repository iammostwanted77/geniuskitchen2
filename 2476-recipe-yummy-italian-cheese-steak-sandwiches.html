Ingredients of Italian Cheese Steak Sandwiches

  2. Prepare 1 tbsp of olive oil.
  3. You need 1 jar of (1 lb. 8 oz.) Ragu® Chunky Pasta Sauce.
  4. Prepare 1/8 tsp of crushed red pepper flakes.
  5. It's 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese, (about 4 oz.).

Italian Cheese Steak Sandwiches step by step

  1. Season steaks, if desired, with salt and ground black pepper..
  2. In large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat and brown steaks, turning occasionally, 4 minutes or until steaks are almost done. Remove steaks and set aside..
  3. In same skillet, stir in Pasta Sauce and red pepper flakes. Cook, stirring frequently, 2 minutes. Return steaks to skillet; top steaks with cheese. Cook covered over medium heat 2 minutes or until cheese is melted. To serve, arrange steaks on platter. Pour sauce around steaks and garnish, if desired, with chopped fresh parsley..
