Ingredients of Eggplant Parmesan

  1. It's 1 medium of eggplant, peeled and cut into 1/4-inch slices, (1 1/2lbs.).
  2. It's 1 of cooking spray.
  3. Prepare 1/3 cup of finely shredded Parmesan cheese.
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup of Progresso dry bread crumbs (any flavor).
  5. It's 2 tsp of olive or vegetable oil.
  6. It's 1 cup of spaghetti sauce.
  7. Prepare 1 1/2 cup of shredded reduced-fat mozzarella cheese (6 oz.).

Eggplant Parmesan step by step

  1. Set oven control to broil. Generously spray both sides of each eggplant slice with cooking spray. Place on rack in boiler pan. Broiled with tops 4 to 5 inches from heat about 10 minutes, turning once, until tender..
  2. While eggplant is broiling, mix Parmesan cheese and bread crumbs; toss with oil..
  3. Heat spaghetti sauce in 1-quart saucepan over medium heat about 2 minutes, stirring occasionally, until heated through. Remove from heat; cover to keep warm..
  4. Sprinkle 1 cup of the mozzarella cheese over eggplant slices. Spoon bread crumb mixture over cheese. Broil about 1 minute or until cheese is melted and crumbs are brown. Top eggplant with spaghetti sauce and remaining 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese..
