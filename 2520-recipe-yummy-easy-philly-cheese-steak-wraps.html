easy philly cheese steak wraps step by step

  1. slice & saute peppers n onions keep warm.
  2. in a large pan cook your steak ums as you cook them chop them up a bit with fork.
  3. add your cream cheese into the steak ums . cook until melted..
  4. warm your wraps or soft tortillas.
  5. spread Mayo on your wraps, fill with steak mixture, top with the sauted peppers n onions.. roll up and enjoy!.
