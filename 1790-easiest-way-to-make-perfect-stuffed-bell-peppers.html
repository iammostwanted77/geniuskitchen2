Ingredients of Stuffed bell peppers

  1. Prepare 6 large of peppers with tops cut off or 4 peppers that are cut in half. I use green, red, yellow, and orange sometimes.
  2. Prepare 1 lb of lean ground beef.
  3. You need 1/2 cup of chopped onion.
  4. You need 1 clove of garlic minced.
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp of Emeril's bam burger seasoning.
  6. Prepare 1 can of 15 oz tomato sauce with garlic.
  7. You need 2 cup of cooked rice.
  8. Prepare 1/2 can of Petite diced tomatoes drained.
  9. You need 1 dash of salt and pepper.
  10. You need 1 can of 15 oz brooks mild or spicy chili beans drained.
  11. It's 2 cup of shredded cheddar cheese.

Stuffed bell peppers instructions

  1. Pre heat oven to 375° F Remove seeds and rinse peppers throughly with water.
  2. Salt and pepper the insides of the peppers and place in a baking dish add 1/2 cup water to the bottom of dish.
  3. cook ground beef with onion, garlic, salt, pepper, and seasoning.
  4. drain beef and add 1/2 can of sauce, beans, tomatoes, rice, and 1 cup of cheese mix throughly.
  5. stuff peppers with mixture top with remaining sauce and cheese.
  6. cover and bake for 25 minutes then uncover and bake for another 25 minutes.
