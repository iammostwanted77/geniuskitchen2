Veal and Eggplant Pizza step by step

  1. Prepare veal patties as directed on package..
  2. Heat greased grill to medium heat..
  3. Use vegetable peeler to slice through eggplant skin in ribbons - your end result is a striped eggplant. Cut eggplant crosswise into 1 inch thick slices, yielding 6-8 slices..
  4. Reserve 1 tbsp dressing for later, and brush both sides of eggplant slices with remaining vinaigrette..
  5. Grill eggplant for 8 minutes and turn. Top with pizza sauce and cheese. Grill about 5 minutes until cheese is melted..
  6. Top each veal patty with an eggplant slice, sprinkle with fresh basil and drizzle with reserved dressing..
