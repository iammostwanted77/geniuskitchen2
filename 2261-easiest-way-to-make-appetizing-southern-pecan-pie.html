Ingredients of Southern Pecan Pie

  1. It's 4 tbsp of butter.
  2. It's 3/4 cup of brown sugar.
  3. You need 1 tbsp of all-purpose flour.
  4. You need 1/4 tsp of salt.
  5. You need 3/4 cup of cane syrup.
  6. Prepare 3 of eggs.
  7. You need 1 tsp of vanilla extract.
  8. You need 1 cup of pecan halves.
  9. Prepare 1 of 9-inch pie crust.

Southern Pecan Pie step by step

  1. preheat oven 325°F.
  2. cream the butter, sugar, flour, and salt until smooth.
  3. add the syrup, eggs, and vanilla, then fold in the pecans.
  4. pour the filling into the pie crust and bake for 45-50 minutes or until firm to the touch.
