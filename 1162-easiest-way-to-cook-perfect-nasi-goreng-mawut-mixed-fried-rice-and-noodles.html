Ingredients of Nasi Goreng Mawut (Mixed Fried Rice and Noodles)

  1. Prepare 2 of plates cooked white rice (leftover rice, put in the refrigerator for a night, is recommended).
  2. It's 100 gr of yellow noodles, soaked in warm water for 5-10 minutes, drained.
  3. You need 1 bunch of mustard greens, cut 3 cm.
  4. It's 150 gr of boneless chicken breast, boiled, shredded, set aside.
  5. You need 3 Tbsp of sweet soy sauce.
  6. You need to taste of salt and mushrooms bouillon.
  7. It's 2 Tbsp of vegetable oil.
  8. Prepare of Ground spices:.
  9. You need 3 cloves of garlic.
  10. It's 2 pcs of candle nuts 4 chilli peppers.
  11. It's 2 of bird eye chilli peppers (optional).
  12. You need 1/4 tsp of white peppercorn.
  13. Prepare of Complements.
  14. You need 2 of sunny side up eggs.
  15. You need of some rice crackers.
