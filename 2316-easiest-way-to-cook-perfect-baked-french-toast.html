Baked French Toast instructions

  1. Tear bread into chunks and spread evenly in pan.
  2. Mix eggs, milk, cream, sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla. Pour evenly over bread..
  3. Cover tightly and place in fridge over night or bake immediately in oven at 350° for 1 hour and 30 minutes..
