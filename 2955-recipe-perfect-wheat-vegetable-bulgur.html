Wheat vegetable bulgur.

You can have Wheat vegetable bulgur using 18 ingredients and 8 steps. Here is how you cook that.

Ingredients of Wheat vegetable bulgur

  1. Prepare 500 gram of coarse wheat bulgur, washed.
  2. You need 1 cup of chopped aubergine in cubes.
  3. Prepare 1 cup of chopped potatoes in cubes.
  4. It's 1 cup of finely chopped carrots.
  5. Prepare 1 cup of finely chopped white onions.
  6. You need 3 of green or red bell peppers if desired.
  7. Prepare 25 grams of butter.
  8. It's of grated parmesan cheese.
  9. Prepare of vegetable oil for frying aubergine and potatoes.
  10. It's of black pepper half a teaspoon.
  11. Prepare 10 grams of salt.
  12. Prepare 5 grams of red cayenne pepper.
  13. It's 1 clove of garlic.
  14. It's 750 ml of water.
  15. Prepare half of a teaspoon cumin powder.
  16. You need of parsley to garnish.
  17. Prepare 1 of TBP tomato paste.
  18. Prepare 1 TSP of chicken base.

Wheat vegetable bulgur step by step

  1. Chop the aubergine and potatoes into cubes..
  2. Fry the aubergine till brown the fry the potatoes..
  3. Put the finely chopped white onions in a pot with the butter. Fry then add the aubergine, potatoes, carrots, garlic and chilli peppers. Add the bulgur..
  4. Add the salt, cumin, black and red peppers..
  5. Pour over 750 ml of water to barely cover the ingredients in the pan..
  6. Add tomato paste and chicken base. Let the water boil,then reduce the heat and cover the pan..
  7. Turn off the heat after 15 min. Keep covered for 20 minutes..
  8. Serve hot with grated parmesan cheese or cooking cream on top. Garnish with parsley. You can serve with yoghurt side dish or kefir..
