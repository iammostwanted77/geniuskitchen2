Taco goulash instructions

  1. Cook sausage in pan than add burger and cook till almost done.
  2. Add onion and red pepper cook till burger is done.
  3. Mix the taco seasoning in than add 2 cans of stewed tomatoes (we break em up a little bit) add corn.
  4. Cook your noodles till almost done.... still a little chewy.
  5. Drain noodles and combine all of it for a least a couple minutes or until noodles are done.
