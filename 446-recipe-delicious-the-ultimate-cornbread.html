Ingredients of The ultimate cornbread

The ultimate cornbread instructions

  1. Gather all your ingredients together as preheat the oven to 350f/ 180c.
  2. Mix the cornmeal, sugar, flour, baking powder, baking Soda, and salt in a bowl and make a well in the center.
  3. Whisk the eggs, milk, melted butter, buttermilk whisk well.
  4. Stir in the dry ingredients and make a thick paste Put into a baking loaf.
  5. Bake for 40-45 minutes in the center of the oven till toothpick comes out clean Best served on same day.
