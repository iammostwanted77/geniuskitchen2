Sunshine's cranberry sauce instructions

  1. In a medium pot, add water, lemon juice, sugar and cranberries, mix and bring to a soft boil, cook for 8 to 10 minutes, stirring till time is up, set to side.
  2. Now mix the lemon juice, cold tap water and gelatine together in a small bowl or cup. let set a minute..
  3. Add gelatine mix to cranberries mix.now put the cranberry sauce in a container to mole.(I used a empty sour cream container).
