oven goulash step by step

  1. preheat oven 450°F Fahrenheit.
  2. cook onion peppers garlic sausage bacon spices.
  3. add wine to meat.
  4. cook the elbows as directions on the box.
  5. after meat is cooked add tomatoes after elbows are done add the sauces and puree.
  6. add all the ingredients together except olives cheeses.
  7. put a layer of goulash on bottom of pan add olive and parmesan cheese layer of goulash add Colby Jack cheese on top bake 20 minutes.
