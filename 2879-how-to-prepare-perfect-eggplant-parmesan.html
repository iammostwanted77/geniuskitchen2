Eggplant Parmesan step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 400° degrees.
  2. Heat 3 cup vegatable oil to mid/high heat in medium size pot..
  3. Cut eggplant to 1/4 to 1/2 inch medallions size..
  4. Let eggplants soak in water for about 15 min..
  5. Get out 3 bowls..
  6. Bowl 1 mix your flower, salt, and white pepper. Bowl 2 scramble eggs and milk. Bowl 3 combined italin bread crumes, garlic powdet, ouion powder, and italian seasoning..
  7. Take your cut eggplant (one slice at a time) and cover on both side bowl 1, 2, and 3 (in that order)..
  8. Put coated eggplant in the hot oil till golden brown on both sides..
  9. Place on cookie sheet..
  10. Top with sauce and cheese..
  11. Place in oven 5 to 15 minutes or until cheese is melted..
