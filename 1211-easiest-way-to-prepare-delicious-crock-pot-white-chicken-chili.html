Ingredients of crock pot white chicken chili

  1. It's 4 can of 15 oz S & W white beans.
  2. You need 1 can of 15 oz del monte petite cut diced tomatoes.
  3. Prepare 1 can of 4 oz ortega fire roasted green chilies,diced.
  4. It's 2 packages of McCormick white chicken chili seasoning.
  5. Prepare 3 cup of water.
  6. Prepare 2 each of large chicken breast boneless/skinless 8oz ea.

crock pot white chicken chili instructions

  1. add beans, tomatoes, chiles, both packages of seasoning mix and three cups of water. Stir ingredients to mix well.
  2. lay chicken on top of beans and seasoning mix. Put lid on crackpot..
  3. turn on high. cook 3-4 hours, or 6-8 hours on low setting..
  4. before serving, shred chicken and stir.
