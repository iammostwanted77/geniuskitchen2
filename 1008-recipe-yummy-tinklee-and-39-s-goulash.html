Ingredients of Tinklee's Goulash

  1. Prepare 1 1/2 lb of hamburger.
  2. It's 2 tbsp of Onion powder.
  3. It's 2 tbsp of Garlic powder.
  4. You need 3 tbsp of Italian seasoning.
  5. Prepare 1/4 tsp of Black pepper.
  6. You need 1/4 tsp of cumin.
  7. You need 2 can of Tomato sauce 15 oz each.
  8. It's 2 can of Olives 6oz each.
  9. It's 1/3 cup of Olive juice (from can of olives).
  10. Prepare 1 lb of Elbow macaroni.

Tinklee's Goulash instructions

  1. Brown hamburger over medium-high heat, add 1 tablespoon onion powder, 1 tablespoon garlic powder, 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning, when hamburger is done cooking drain grease if there is any.
  2. Boil macaroni according to directions on package and drain when done.
  3. Add tomato sauce to hamburger, add the rest of all the seasonings, cook over medium low heat.
  4. Add olives and olive juice and continue cooking over low heat.
  5. Mix macaroni and hamburger together and serve.
