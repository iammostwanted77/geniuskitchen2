Ingredients of Easy Peasie Super Cheesy Cheesesteak W/Mushrooms

  1. It's 4 slice of Frozen Steaks (Steak-Um's).
  2. It's 3 tbsp of Mild Cheddar Cheese Sauce.
  3. It's 1 can of 6 oz Mushrooms.
  4. Prepare 3 slice of American Cheese.
  5. Prepare 1 of Roll.

Easy Peasie Super Cheesy Cheesesteak W/Mushrooms instructions

  1. Add Frozen steak to frying pan.
  2. As it starts to cook break up steaks with Two spatulas. (Pulling the steak apart with each hand at the same time).
  3. Break up slices of American cheese over top steak.
  4. Drop 4 heaping table spoons of cheddar cheese over steak.
  5. Open can of mushrooms and drain. Place as many mushrooms as desired on top of the cheesesteak.
  6. Mix everything together until cheeses are melted and Mushrooms are cooked.
  7. Add to roll and enjoy! Served with a few chips!!.
