Easy buttery pie crust step by step

  1. Measure and pour flour into bowl..
  2. Add slices of cold butter and stir until mixture turns into crumbs..
  3. Add ice water 1 tbsp at a time and mix until dough forms..
  4. Roll into ball and cover with plastic. Set in fridge overnight or 4 hrs minimum..
  5. When ready to use, roll dough out and brush with egg wash..
