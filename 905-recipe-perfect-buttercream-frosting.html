  3. It's 3 tbsp of Milk or Cream.
  4. You need 1/2 tsp of Pure Vanilla Extract.

Buttercream Frosting step by step

  1. In a stand mixer with the wire attachment, whip the butter until slightly cooled, and turn it to medium speed..
  2. Add the sugar to the butter one cup at a time, and beat it in medium low to medium speed..
  3. Pour the milk or cream one tablespoon at a time..
  4. Gradually, add in vanilla..
