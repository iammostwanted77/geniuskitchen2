North American Goulash instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F.
  2. In a medium pot bring water to a boil..
  3. Add macaroni to pot and boil until the macaroni is slightly undercooked..
  4. Turn off the heat and reserve the macaroni for later..
  5. Chop tomato into large pieces..
  6. Grease a dutch oven with a little bit of butter under a medium heat.
  7. Add onions, peppers, and mushrooms. Cook until tender..
  8. Add the ground turkey and cook until it browns..
  9. Next add the remaining ingredients and pasta to the pot and stir thoroughly..
  10. Bake for 40 minutes..
