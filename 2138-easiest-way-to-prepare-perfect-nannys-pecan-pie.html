  4. You need 1 1/2 cup of chopped pecans.

  10. You need pinch of corn meal (pretty big pinch).

Nanny`s pecan pie instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. To eggs add vanilla and maple beat. Sit to the side..
  3. In a large 4 cup measuring cup or bowl put your syrup, sugar,pecans, salt, corn meal and butter. Mix well.
  4. Add the egg mixture and mix. Side aside..
  5. Place Reynolds wrap around the top of the crust so it won't burn..
  6. Stir pie filling again and divide half into each crust..
  7. Place in oven. Cook for about 40 minutes..
  8. It needs to sit and cool for at least 3 hours. Can be frozen for later..Make sure you take the wrap off before serving..
