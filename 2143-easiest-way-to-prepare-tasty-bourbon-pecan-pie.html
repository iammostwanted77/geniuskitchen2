Ingredients of Bourbon Pecan Pie

  1. It's 3 of eggs, large.
  2. You need 3 1/2 oz of brown sugar.
  3. You need 6 oz of golden syrup.
  4. You need 4 tbsp of butter, melted and slightly cooled.
  5. Prepare 1 1/2 tbsp of bourbon.
  6. You need 1 1/2 tsp of vanilla extract.
  7. It's 1/4 tsp of salt.
  8. Prepare 10 oz of pecans (8 oz chopped/2 oz whole).
