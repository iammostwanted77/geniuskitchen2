Ingredients of Gluten free Pecan Pie Brownie

  1. Prepare of Crust.
  2. You need 250 ml of Gluten Free Flour.
  3. It's 2 ml of Xanthan Gum.
  4. Prepare 60 ml of Light Caramel Sugar.
  5. Prepare 120 g of Cold Margarine.
  6. It's of Chocolate Brownie.
  7. Prepare 100 ml of Sugar.
  8. You need 2 of Large Eggs.
  9. It's 90 g of Semi Sweet Dark Chocolate.
  10. You need 90 g of Margarine.
  11. You need 65 ml of Cocoa Powder.
  12. Prepare 125 ml of Gluten Free Flour.
  13. Prepare 1 ml of Xanthan Gum.
  14. Prepare 2 ml of Baking Powder.
  15. It's 1 ml of Salt.
  16. It's of Pecan Pie.
  17. Prepare 50 ml of Maple Syrup.
  18. Prepare 100 ml of Caramel Sugar.
  19. It's 75 ml of Orley Whip.
  20. Prepare 50 g of Margarine.
  21. It's 1 ml of Salt.
  22. Prepare 1 ml of Cinnamon.
  23. You need 1 ml of Vanilla Essence.
  24. You need 300 ml of Pecan Nuts (Roasted and Chopped).

Gluten free Pecan Pie Brownie step by step

  1. For Crust: Combine the flour, brown sugar, xanthan gum and margarine until it comes together..
  2. With a spoon or spatula evenly spread out the dough onto a greased baking tray..
  3. Bake for 20min on 170°C. Leave to cool..
  4. For Brownies: Beat the eggs and sugar together until pale..
  5. Melt the chocolate and margarine together and add to egg mixture. Mix well..
  6. Sieve in the cocoa, flour, salt and baking powder and fold in..
  7. Evenly spread mixture over crust and bake for 20-25min on 170°C. Leave to cool..
  8. In a sauce pan, mix the syrup, sugar, Orley Whip, margarine, salt, cinnamon and vanilla. Heat the mixture to 118°C..
  9. Mix in the Pecan nuts and stir well..
  10. Pour over brownie and leave to cool completely before cutting up and serving..
