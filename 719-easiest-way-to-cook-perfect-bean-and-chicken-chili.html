Bean and Chicken Chili step by step

  1. In a large pot, heat oil over medium-high heat; brown chicken and transfer to a plate..
  2. Add carrot, onion, peppers, chili powder, oregano and cumin to pot; cook stirring for 5 minutes or until vegetables are softened..
  3. Add tomatoes, kidney beans and tomato paste; bring to a boil. Reduce heat and boil gently for 10 minutes or until thickened..
  4. Whisk flour into milk; gradually stir into pot..
  5. Add corn, cooked chicken; simmer, stirring until thickened..
  6. Season with salt & pepper..
  7. Sprinkle individual servings with cheese..
