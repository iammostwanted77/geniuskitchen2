Ingredients of Pecan pie bread pudding

  1. You need 1 loaf of day old French bread.
  2. Prepare 2 1/2 cup of milk.
  3. It's 1 cup of half and half.
  4. It's 4 of eggs (lightly beaten ).
  5. You need 1 tbsp of vanilla.
  6. It's 1/8 tsp of salt.
  7. You need 1/2 cup of butter (softened ).
  8. You need 1 cup of chopped pecans.
  9. It's 1 cup of granulated sugar.
  10. Prepare 1 1/2 cup of brown sugar, packed.

Pecan pie bread pudding instructions

  1. Preheat oven 350Â°f.
  2. Cube bread, put in large bowl and set aside.
  3. Beat eggs, milk, half and half, sugar, salt, and vanilla together. Pour over bread, and let sit 5-10 minutes.
  4. In another bowl combine, with a fork, butter, brown sugar, and pecans. Will be like wet sand when done..
  5. Pour half of the bread mixture into 8x8 pan.
  6. Top with half of the pecan mixture.
  7. Spoon remaining bread mixture over and top with remaining pecan mixture. Press into pan slightly. Pan will be really full.
  8. Place on pan on cookie sheet (with raised edges) in case of boil over. Bake for 45-55 minutes. Center will be slightly wiggly but will set as it cools..
