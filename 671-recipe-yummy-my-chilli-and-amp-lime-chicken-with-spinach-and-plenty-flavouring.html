My Chilli & Lime Chicken with Spinach and plenty flavouring. ðŸ’œ instructions

  1. Slice the chicken breasts into lengths. Then add to a bowl with the mix of cornflour, chilli and pepper. Coat chicken strips.leave for 10 minutes..
  2. Cut the onion into small pieces. Also the 3 shallots. Add all the paste ingredients in a blender and blend them all to a smooth pulp. Add to a bowl..
  3. Paste all mixed. Set aside.
  4. Add the oil to the pan heat up then add the chicken pieces, in batches. Fry until browned and crisp fry turning often for 4 minutes each batch. Set aside..
  5. In the same pan if there is no oil add 1 tbls olive oil heat up then add the pulp let fry on high turning it all over often fry for 3 minutes..
  6. Next add the spinach and 2 or 3 full small chilli peppers and fold the spinach in when it becomes soggy after a minute then add the chicken cook all together turning until heated through about 2 minutes..
  7. Seere with some boiled Basmati rice with pepper and 1 tsp chilli oil mixed in tbe rice. Or mix 1 tsp garlic oil in the rice..
