Ingredients of Goulash for the People!

  1. Prepare 3 cups of water.
  2. You need 2 (14.5 cans) of diced tomatoes.
  3. It's 1 (29 oz) of can tomato sauce.
  4. You need 12 oz of tomato paste.
  5. Prepare 3 of bay leaves.
  6. It's 2 lbs of ground beef.
  7. You need 4 cloves of garlic.
  8. Prepare 1 Tbs of Italian seasoning.
  9. Prepare 2 Tbs of Worcestershire sauce.
  10. Prepare 1 lbs of elbow macaroni.
  11. Prepare to taste of Salt and pepper.
  12. It's 1/3 cup of white sugar.

Goulash for the People! step by step

  1. Brown ground beef in Dutch oven. Add onion and garlic when browned and cook until translucent..
  2. Add tomato sauce, tomato paste and stir. Then add diced tomatoes and water. Stir then add seasonings and sauce. Stir then add bay leaves..
  3. Bring to boil and simmer 30 minutes..
  4. Now drink a beer or some wine whichever you prefer.
  5. Remove Bay Leaves and add pasta. Boil for 8-10 minutes until pasta is tender..
  6. Have another drink you deserve it, and stir when needed..
  7. Done !.
  8. Let sit a few minutes to cool before serving. Cook your favorite garlic bread now..
