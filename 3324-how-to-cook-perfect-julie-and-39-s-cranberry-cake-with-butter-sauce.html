Julie's Cranberry Cake With Butter Sauce step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Mix dry ingredients for cake. Stir in milk, butter until smooth. Mix in cranberries..
  2. Pour batter into greased 9x9 inch baking pan. Bake at 375°F for 20-30 minutes. Watch carefully-don't overvalue!.
  3. For the sauce: mix sauce ingredients except vanilla in saucepan. Heat to boiling and simmer until syrupy. Remove from heat and serve warm over cake..
  4. Can double recipe to make 9x13 pan, if using frozen cranberries thaw first..
