Ingredients of Poached eggs with veggies and shoots

  1. You need 2 of eggs.
  2. You need 1 tsp of lemon juice.
  3. Prepare 1 slice of Ezekiel bread.
  4. You need 1/2 cup of mushrooms, chopped finely.
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup of yellow or red pepper, chopped finely.
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup of daikon shoots.
  7. You need of Pepper.
  8. You need of Nutritional yeast.

Poached eggs with veggies and shoots step by step

  1. Pour water in a small sauce pan and bring to a boil. Add lemon juice, reduce heat to med-high Place egg in a bowl and pouring one egg at a time into sauce pan. Cook 2-3 minutes. If you wish to have a your eggs well cooked, leave in boiling water for an extra 2 minutes.
  2. Toast slice bread . In a frying pan, add oil of choice (ex. Butter, coconut butter, olive oil Put cut veggies into pan, reduce heat to med. cook 3 -4 minutes. Sprinkle nutritional yeast on top of veggies..
  3. Place bread onto plate, half veggies. With a slotted spoon remove eggs from water place on top of bread and veggies. Place daikon shoots on top of eggs and remaining veggies. Add pepper.

Push the vegetables to the side of the pan to create four spaces for the eggs. Crack eggs directly into the holes, being careful not to break the yolk. Cook until eggs are done, but the yolk is still soft. After removing from oven, add salt and pepper to taste. Combine pea shoots and lemon juice in a food processor; pulse to blend.
