Ingredients of Cindys Summer Slow Cooker Pot Roast

  1. Prepare of main dish.
  2. You need 3 lb of chuck roast.
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp of flour.
  4. Prepare 3 tbsp of olive oil.
  5. You need 1 of large onion chopped.
  6. You need 4 clove of garlic chopped.
  7. You need 2 of stock celery chopped.
  8. It's 4 of carrots rough chopped.
  9. It's 1 lb of green beans.
  10. Prepare 3 lb of new potatos.
  11. It's 4 cup of water.
  12. Prepare 1/4 cup of cindys spice rub (recipe at end).

Cindys Summer Slow Cooker Pot Roast step by step

  1. Set up a safe place outside.
  2. Turn Slow Cooker on high.
  3. Add all the rest of ingredients and put lid on and cook 6 hours or longer if you like everything softer..
  4. Rub meat on both sides with spice rub and flour.
  5. Brown meat on both sides in olive oil.
  6. Cindys spice rub!!!.
  7. Chili powder 4 Tablespoon.
  8. Paprika 6 Tablespoons.
  9. Granulated garlic 2 Tablespoon.
  10. Lemon pepper 2 Tablespoons.
  11. Tomato, chicken bullion 2 Tablespoon.
  12. Cumin 1 Tablespoon. mix together save leftovers in jar.
