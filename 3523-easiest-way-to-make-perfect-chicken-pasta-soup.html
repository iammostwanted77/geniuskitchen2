Chicken pasta soup step by step

  1. Fry onion and garlic slightly. Add chicken pieces and brown nicely..
  2. Add water, salt and spices and simmer for 30 mins to chicken is tender. Remove chicken with a slotted spoon, keeping the chicken stock in the pan. Let chicken cool a little..
  3. Now add the your vegetables and simmer till tender. About 10 mins..
  4. Whilst veg is simmering, de-bone chicken pieces and add the mean to the pot with the simmering veg, along with the pasta. Cook till pasta is done to your liking..
  5. Serve as is, Or if you're like me and love chilli, you can add a dash of chilli sauce to your bowl. Delish!.
