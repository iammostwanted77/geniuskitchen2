pecan pie instructions

  1. mix all ingredients for crust and divide in two halfs.
  2. preheat oven to 350°F.
  3. spray two pie pans.
  4. take crust and smooth out in each pie pan.
  5. now take 2 cups of brown sugar and put 1 cup in a bowl so two bowls with a cup.
  6. now add 3 eggs per bowl.
  7. and add 1 cup of karo syrup to each bowl.
  8. now add 1/3 cup of melted butter per bowl.
  9. stir each bowl till thoroughly mixed.
  10. pour into pie crusts and top with 1 1/2 cups of pecans.
  11. bake for about 75 minutes on 350.
