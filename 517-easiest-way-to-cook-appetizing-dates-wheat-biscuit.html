Dates wheat biscuit step by step

  1. In a bowl whisk the butter till creamy and keep aside, then soak the dates in the hot milk for 15-20mins.After that grind to a paste form in a blender..
  2. Now add the dates paste in the bowl with butter and mix well. Sieve all the dry ingredients and make a dough..
  3. Roll it not too thin nor thick, using a biscuit cutter cut the dough..
  4. Preheat the oven for 10-15mins then place the biscuit in the oven and cook for 10-12mins..
