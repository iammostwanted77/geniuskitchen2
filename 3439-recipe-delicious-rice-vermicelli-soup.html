Ingredients of Rice vermicelli soup

  1. It's 4-5 pcs of rice vermicelli.
  2. It's 200 gram of minced chicken.
  3. Prepare 4 of eggs.
  4. It's 1 pinch of salt.
  5. You need 1 pinch of sugar.
  6. Prepare 1 of teaspn chicken powder.
  7. You need 1 of tblspn corn starch.
  8. It's 2 tbspn of pepper.
  9. You need 10 pcs of fishball.
  10. You need 5 tbspn of vegetable oil.
  11. Prepare 6 cloves of minced garlic.

Rice vermicelli soup step by step

  1. In medium heat, put the minced garlic..
  2. After the garlic became golden brown and smelled good, put minced chicken, fish ball along with the eggs..
  3. Stir it all well until 1 minute. Then put pepper, salt,sugar and chicken powder until mixed well..
  4. After that, put around 1.5 ltr boiled water or chicken broth into it..
  5. Wait until 2 minutes, then put corn starch and stir it well..
  6. Prepare 1 bowl with rice vermicelli in it, then put all soup into the bowl and put the sunny side up egg on the top of the soup..
