Chocolate Oreo Balls step by step

  1. Remove the cream that is sandwiched between the Oreo biscuits and keep it aside..
  2. In a grinder container, add the Oreo biscuits (after removing the cream in between), sugar, butter and grind it into a smooth powder..
  3. Add the milk and cashews and knead it very well..
  4. If you like the flavour of coffee. You may add a bit of coffee powder in it. That is totally optional. I have not used it. That is why I haven't add it to the ingredients..
  5. Make small balls out of the dough and refrigerate it for atleast 10min to solidify it more..
  6. Chop the chocolate and take it in a bowl. Add some melted butter and mix it well..
  7. Heat it in a microwave oven for 30secs in full power..
  8. Whisk it very well with the help of a spoon so that the heat spreads everywhere evenly and you get the melted chocolate..
  9. If you think you may heat it again for few seconds and again whisk it..
  10. Don't burn the chocolate..
  11. Take the cream of the Oreo biscuits that was kept aside in a bowl and mix it with a teaspoon of milk..
  12. Add it in the melted chocolate..
  13. Mix it very well..
  14. Now remove the Oreo balls from the fridge and dip them in this melted chocolate mixture..
  15. Garnish them with colourful sprinklers and again refrigerate it for atleast 2hours or until the chocolate thickens..
  16. Remove it from the refrigerator and enjoy it with your family and friends..
