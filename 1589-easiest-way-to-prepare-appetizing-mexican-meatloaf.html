Mexican Meatloaf step by step

  1. Mix the Moist Ingredients in a small mixing bowl..
  2. Add the Dry Ingredients to a medium mixing bowl..
  3. Pour the Moist Ingredients over the Dry Ingredients and knead until well mixed..
  4. Transfer the mixture to a foil-lined baking sheet and form into a loaf shape. Cover with plastic wrap and place in the refrigerator for 30 minutes..
  5. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F..
  6. Shortly before the meatloaf is done in the fridge, mix together the crust ingredients in a bowl..
  7. Remove the meatloaf from the fridge and discard the plastic wrap..
  8. Pat the crust onto the meatloaf and place in oven..
  9. After 60 minutes at 350, increase temperature to 425 and cook for another 20 minutes..
  10. Shortly before the meatloaf is done in the oven, mix the Crema ingredients together in a bowl..
  11. Spoon crema over slices of meatloaf before serving. Serve with Mexican rice and/or tortilla chips with your choice of dip..
