Ingredients of Slow cooked pork tenderloin

  1. It's 3 lbs of pork tenderloin.
  2. Prepare 1 tsp of salt.
  3. It's 1 tsp of black pepper.
  4. Prepare 1 tsp of onion powder.
  5. You need 1 tsp of cumin.
  6. It's 1 tsp of garlic powder.
  7. It's 4 tbls of olive oil.
  8. It's 3 tbls of basalmic vinegar.
  9. Prepare 5 of gloves garlic.
  10. It's 1 tbls of italion seasoning.
  11. You need 1/2 cup of chicken stock.

Slow cooked pork tenderloin step by step

  1. Pat dry the tenderloin with paper towel..
  2. Mix salt, black pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and cumin in small bowl and rub onto the pork tenderloin..
  3. Add 1 tbls olive oil in cast iron pan. Sear all sides of tenderloin in a cast iron pan. Few minutes each..
  4. Pour chicken stock in slow cooker then place the loin in there...
  5. Mix remaining oil, basalmic vinegar and gloves in blender until thick and brush onto the loin..
  6. Cook on low for 5 to 6 hours..
